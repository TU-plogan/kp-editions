MUNDATTAFAL, a town of Hindustan, in the Mahratta territories, in the province of Khandesh, being situated on a small island formed by the Nerbuddah, sixty-five [7:15:592]miles south-south-east from Oojain. Long. 76. 17. E. Lat. 22. 25. N.
