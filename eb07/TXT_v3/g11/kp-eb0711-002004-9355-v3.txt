GUBEN, a city of Prussia, the capital of the circle of the same name, in the province of Frankfort. It stands on the river Neisse, which is navigable to the Oder, is surrounded with walls, and contains 850 houses, and 7960 inhabitants, who carry on various manufactures in woollen [7:11:21] and linen goods, and other commodities. Long. 12. 40. E. Lat. 51. 58. N.
