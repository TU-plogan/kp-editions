PAWANGHUR, a fortified town of Hindustan, in the Maliratta territories, province of Bcjapoor, and district of Parnella, of which it is the capital. It is considered as a place of great strength, and is forty-two miles south by west from Merritch. Long. 74. 20. E. Lat. 16. 52. N.
