PETLAD, a town of Hindustan, in the province of Gujerat, and district of Broach. It is sixteen miles north-east from Cambay. The inhabitants are here subjected to the servitude of acting as guides to travellers, from which only a certain caste, the Dhers, are exempted. Long. 73. E. Lat. 22. 27. N.
