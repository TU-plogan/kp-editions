EINDHOVEN, a city of the province osNorth Brabant, in the Netherlands. It stands at the junction of the rivers Gender and Dommel, and contains 390 houses, and 2975 inhabitants. Long. 5. 23. 20. E. Lat. 51. 25.26. N. It is the capital of a circle of the same name, divided into seven cantons, which contain 86,560 inhabitants.
