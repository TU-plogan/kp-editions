EBERN, a bailiwick of the circle of the Lower Maine, in the kingdom of Bavaria, extending over seventy-one square miles. It contains one city, one market-town, and 11,400 inhabitants. It is watered by the river Baunach, has excellent pasture, and moderate c< .. land. The capital, of the same name, contains 207 houses, and 1100 inhabitants employed in making glass and porcelain wares.
