ALTENBRUCH, a market-town on the Werne, in the principality of Verden, in the kingdom of Hanover. It contains 366 houses and 2319 inhabitants; and has a small harbour, from whence it carries on trade in corn, fruit, and cattle. It is in long. 8. 51. 3. Îˆ. and lat. 53. 50. 5. N.
