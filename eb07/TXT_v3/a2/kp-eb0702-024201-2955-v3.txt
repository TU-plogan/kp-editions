AGATHA, St, a market town on the Austrian principality of the Siebenbirgen, situated on the river Hartbache, with one Greek and one Lutheran church. The people arc employed as coopers, shoemakers, and hosiers.

Agatha, Santa, a town on the banks of a small river in the province Calabria Ulteriore, in the kingdom of Naples, with a castle, and 1127 inhabitants.
