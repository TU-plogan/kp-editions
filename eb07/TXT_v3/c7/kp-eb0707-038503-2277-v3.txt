COTERIE, a term adopted from the French trading associations or partnerships, where each person advances bis quota of stock, and receives his proportion of gain; and it retains its original meaning when applied to little assemblies or companies associated for mirth and social enjoyment, where each one furnishes his quota of pleasantry.
