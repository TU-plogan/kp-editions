CRANBORNE, a small town of the county of Dorset, ninety-three miles from London. The parish is extensive, including a large circuit, formerly a royal chase, but recently disforested and cultivated. The inhabitants amounted in 1801 to 1402, in 1811 to 1611, and in 1821 to 1823.
