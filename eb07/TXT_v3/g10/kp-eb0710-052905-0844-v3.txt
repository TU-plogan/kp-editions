GIURGEWO, a city of the Turkish province of Nicooli. It stands on the northern bank of the Danube, and in the principality of Wallachia, but the garrison is Turkish. It has a strong fortress, and the city itself is fortified. The inhabitants amount to 7000, Greeks, Armenians, and Wal-lacÄ§ians, with very few Turks, excepting the military, who chiefly reside within the fortress of Turn, which commands the city.
