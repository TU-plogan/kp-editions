GARDELEGEN, a city of the Prussian province of Saxony, the capital of a circle of the same name. It is situated on the river Milde, is surrounded with walls, and contains 540 houses, with 4650 inhabitants, who carry on linen, woollen, and cotton manufactures, as well as breweries and distilleries. Long. 14. 15. E. Lat. 52. 32. N.
