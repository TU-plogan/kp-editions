GOAND, a mountainous district of Hindustan, in the province of Gundwana, intersected by the Mahanuddy river. The inhabitants are very uncivilized, and boast of never having been subdued, though they are rendered tributary by the rajah of Nagpoor. The country is poor, and there are no good roads.
