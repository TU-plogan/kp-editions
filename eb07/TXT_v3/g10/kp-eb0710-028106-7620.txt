GAD, a Jewish prophet, the seer or domestic prophet of King David, and his adviser in all matters of importance.

Gad, in Ancient Geography, a district of the Transjordan Palestine, situated between Gilead and the kingdom of Bashan on the north, and the kingdom of the Amorite* on the south, having the Jordan on the west, and bounded by various tribes on the east. It is so called from a tribe of that name.
