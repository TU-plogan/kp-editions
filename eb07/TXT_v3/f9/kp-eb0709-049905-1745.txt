FELEGYAZA, a city of Hungary, in the circle of the Hither Danube, where the archives of the circle are preserved, and all the legal processes are carried on. It is also the head quarters of the army. It contains 1028 houses and 9405 inhabitants. Long. 19. 45. E. Lat. 46. 43. N.
