FISSURES, in Geology, certain interruptions, which divide the several strata of which the body of our globe is composed, in a horizontal or parallel manner.

Fissure of the Bones, in Surgery, is when they are divided either transversely or longitudinally, not quite through, but cracked like glass, by any external force.
