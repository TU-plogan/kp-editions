IGUALADA, a town of Spain, in the province of Catalonia, with 12,000 inhabitants. It is situated near the celebrated monastery of Montserrat, and has a strong castle, which was long occupied by the French during the Peninsular war. It has a very large manufactory of firearms. Lat. 4L 35. N.
