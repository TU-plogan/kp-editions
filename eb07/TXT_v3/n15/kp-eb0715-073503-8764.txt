NARNOUL, a town and district of Hindustan, in the province of Agra, being situated principally between the 28th and 29th degrees of north latitude. It belongs to several independent chiefs. The principal towns are Narnoul and Rewary. Narnoul, the capital, and the residence of an independent chief, is situated in long. 76. 8. E. and lat. 28. 4. N.
