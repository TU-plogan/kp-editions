DIEY, an arrondissement of the department of Vosges, in France, extending over 530 square miles, divided into nine cantons, and containing a population of 83,712 persons, in 109 communes.

Diey, St, a city in the department of the Vosges, in France, the capital of the arrondissement, and situated in a mountainous and woody district on the banks of the Meurthe. It contains 830 houses and 5346 inhabitants. Long. 7.1. E. Lat. 48. 20. N.
