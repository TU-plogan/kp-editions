TABAS, a city of Persia, in the province of Khorassan. It is situated on the road from Herat to Yezd, 337 miles from the former, and 150 from the latter. It stands within a range of mountains, and contains 20,000 inhabitants. Tabas is also the name of a village in Anatolia.
