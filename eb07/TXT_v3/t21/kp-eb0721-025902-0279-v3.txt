TICAO Isle, one of the Philippine Islands, situated due south of the large island of Luzon, about twenty-eight miles in length by seven in average breadth. Here the Spanish galleon used to take in water and provisions before setting sail for Acapulco. Long. 123. 40. E. Lat. 12. 36. N.
