TAULLAH Mhokee, a small village of Hindustan, in the province of Lahore, nine miles north of Nadone, the principal town of the Kaungrah country. It contains a temple highly esteemed by the credulous Hindus, who believe that a volcanic flame which issues from the side of a mountain in the neighbourhood is an emanation from the divinity. Long. 75. 45. E. Lat. 32. 5. N.
