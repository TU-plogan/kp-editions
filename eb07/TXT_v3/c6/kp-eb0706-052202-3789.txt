CHIERI, a'city of Italy, in the province of Turin, and kingdom of Sardinia. It is well buììt, and contains several churches, monasteries, and nunneries, with 10,100 inhabitants, who are employed in manufacturing cotton, silk, and linen goods. It is a great mart for raw silk. Long. 7. 40. E. Lat. 44. 53. N.
