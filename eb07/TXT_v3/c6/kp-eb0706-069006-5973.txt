CIBORIUM, in ecclesiastical writers, the covering for the altar. This covering is supported by four high columns, and forms a kind of tent for the eucharist in the Catholic churches. Some authors call it turris gestatoria, and others pyxis ; but the pyxis is properly the box in which the eucharist is preserved.
