CATTIVELLAUNI, anciently a people of Britain, who occupied the country which is now divided into the counties of Hertford, Bedford, and Bucks. The name of this ancient British people is written in several different ways by Greek and Roman authors, by whom they are called Catti, Cassii, Catticulcani, Cattidudani, Catticludani, &c.
