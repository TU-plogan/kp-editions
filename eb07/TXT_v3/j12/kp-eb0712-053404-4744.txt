The capital, which gives its name to the arrondissement, is a city situated on the river Boutonne, containing 5460 inhabitants, who are partly employed in the manufacture of serges, druggets, and other kinds of woollen goods, and carry on a considerable trade in wine and brandy. There is also an establishment for making gunpowder. It is in long. 0. 40. 5. W. and lat. 45. 55. N.
