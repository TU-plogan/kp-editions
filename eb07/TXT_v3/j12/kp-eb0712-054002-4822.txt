JENEAHGUR, called also Jagncii, a town, and also a strong and celebrated fortress, of Hindustan, province of Bejapoor, situated upon a rock with an extensive tableland. It was built about the year 1443, by Mulik al Ta-jur, generalissimo of the Bhamence sultan. JEN1DSCHE-Bardar, a town of European Turkey, in the circle of Salonica. It is an open place, on the river Bardar, with several mosques and Greek churches, and charitable institutions. It contains 6000 inhabitants, who manufacture cotton goods extensively.
