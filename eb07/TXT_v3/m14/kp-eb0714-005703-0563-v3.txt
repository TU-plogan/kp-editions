MALICOY, an island in the Indian Ocean, between the Laccadive and the Maidive Islands. It is small and low, and the sea breaks with incessant fury on its coasts. It was formerly dependent on one of the Malabar rajahs. Long. 72. 45. E. Lat. 8. 20. N.
