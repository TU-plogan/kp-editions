MELISSA, in fabulous history, a daughter of Melissus king of Crete, who with her sister Amalthoea fed Jupiter with the milk of goats. She first found out the means of collecting honey, and hence it has been fabled that she was changed into a bee, as her name is the Greek word for that insect.
