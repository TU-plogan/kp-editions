MARINE, a general name for the navy of a kingdom or state, as also for the whole economy of naval affairs, or whatever respects the building, rigging, arming, equipping, navigating, and fighting of ships. It comprehends likewise the government of naval armaments, and the state of all the persons employed therein, whether civil or military.
