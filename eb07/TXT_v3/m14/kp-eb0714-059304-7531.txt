MERIDIAN, in Geography, a great circle supposed to be draw n through any part of the surface of the earth, and the two poles, and to which the sun is always perpendicular at noon. In astronomy, this circle is supposed to be in the heavens, and exactly perpendicular to the terrestrial one.
