BETELFAGUI, a town of Asia, in Arabia Felix, famous for the vast quantity of coffee bought and sold there; being the mart to which the country people bring their coffee to sell, and where the Europeans come or send their factors or brokers to purchase it. Long. 57. 20. E. Lat. 15. 40. N.
