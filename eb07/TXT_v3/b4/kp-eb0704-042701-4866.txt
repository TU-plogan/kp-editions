BASILICATA, a province of the kingdom of Naples. It is bounded on the north by Capitanata, on the northeast by Bari and Otranto, on the east by the Bay of Taranto, on the south by Calabria, on the south-west by the Gulf of Policastro, and on the west by Principato-Ulteriore. It extends 3481 square miles over a country including a great part of the Appennines. The population amounts to 378,160 persons, who speak the Calabrian dialect. The capital of the province is Potenza.
