BOOROOJIRD, a flourishing city of Persia, in the province of Irak, situated at the foot of a mountain. It is the chief town of a district peopled by a tribe called Lack, who seldom wander far from the spots to which they are partial, but settle in villages, and employ themselves in the im> provement of their estates. The population is 12,000.
