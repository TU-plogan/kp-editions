BIBLIOMANCY, a kind of divination performed by means of the Bible, otherwise called sortes bibliçoe or sor te sanctorum· It consisted in taking passages of Scripture at hazard, and thence drawing indications concerning the future; as in Augustin’s tolle et lege. It was. much used at the consecration of bishops.
