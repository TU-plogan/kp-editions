BESINGHEIM, a bailiwick, taking its name from the city, which is on the Neckar, where it receives the waters of the Ens. It contains 2310 inhabitants, and is situated in the circle of the Neckar, and kingdom of Wirtemburg. It extends over fifty-seven square miles, contains four cities, five market-towns, and thirteen villages, with 27,286 inhabitants.
