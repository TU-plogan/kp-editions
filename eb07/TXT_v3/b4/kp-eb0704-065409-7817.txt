BITONTO, a city, the see of a bishop, in the province of Bari, in the kingdom of Naples, on the great road from Foggia to Bari. It contains, besides the cathedral, twenty, four churches, and 14,200 inhabitants, who export the excellent wine known by the name of Zagarello.
