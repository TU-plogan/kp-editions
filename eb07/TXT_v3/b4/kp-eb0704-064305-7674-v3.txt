BILSHOFEN, a magistracy in the circle of the Lower

Danube, in the kingdom of Bavaria, comprehending 258 square miles, and containing 22,200 inhabitants. The capital of the district is a small city of the same name at the entrance of the river Bils into the Danube, and contains 270 houses, with about 2000 inhabitants. Long. 13. 6. 40. E. Lat. 48. 28. 30. N.
