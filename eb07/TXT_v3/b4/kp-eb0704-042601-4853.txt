BASILAN, an island lying amid a cluster of small islands off the south-western extremity of Magindanao, about 60 miles in circumference, with a range of mountains in the centre. The island is fertile, and abounds in grain. Wild hogs and deer inhabit the interior, which is watered by considerable streams, but is thinly peopled. It is 18 miles from Magindanao. Long. 121. E. Lat. 5. 50. N.
