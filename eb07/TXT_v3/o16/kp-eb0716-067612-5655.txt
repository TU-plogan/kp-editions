OURCHA, a town of Hindustan, in the province of Allahabad, [7:16:677]and district of Bundelcund. The rajah was formerly a person of great note; but his revenue is now reduced to one lac, and his consequence has sunk in proportion. He is still considered as the head of the petty chiefs of Bundelcund.
