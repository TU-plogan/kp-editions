ORSKAIA, a town of Asiatic Russia, in the government of Orenburg, situated on the Or, about a mile and a half before its junction with the Oural. The Asiatic caravans usually pass this river here in their way to Orenburg. The town of Orenburg was originally built unon this spot. Orskaia is 132 miles east of Orenburg
