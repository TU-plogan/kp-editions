NEUDORF, a city of the circle of Zips, in the province of the Hither Theiss in Hungary, situated on the river Her-nath. It contains one Lutheran and two Catholic churches, 900 houses, and 5250 inhabitants, who are occupied in [7:16:145]making linen goods, and in producing iron and copper wares. Long. 20. 29. 25. E. Lat. 48. 56. 30. N.
