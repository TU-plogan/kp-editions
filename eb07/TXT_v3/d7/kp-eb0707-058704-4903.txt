DAHHI, a large village of Arabia, in the country of Yemen, which has a mosque and the tomb of a saint. The inhabitants are engaged in making bricks and in tanning leather. They also prepare indigo, which is used for dyeing throughout Yemen, that being the favourite colour. Forty-six miles south-east of Loheia.
