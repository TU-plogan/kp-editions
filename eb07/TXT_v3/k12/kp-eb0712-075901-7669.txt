KOPYS, a town of the province Mohilew, in Russia, the capital of a circle of the same name, on the river Dnieper. It contains a Greek, Catholic, a Lutheran, and a Unitarian church, with 3800 inhabitants. Long. 30. 10. E. Lat. 54. 20. N.
