KERCOLANG, an island in the Eastern Seas, and the largest of the Salibabo islands, estimated at from eighty to a hundred miles in circumference, and inhabited chiefly by Mahommedans. It is situated between the 4th and 5th degrees of north latitude, and about 12G. 30. of east longitude.
