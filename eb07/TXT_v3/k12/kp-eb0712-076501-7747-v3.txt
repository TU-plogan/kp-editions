KTEIFA, a small town of Syria, in the pachalic of Damascus, on the borders of the Desert. It is surrounded with walls of sufficient strength to defend it against the travelling Arabs. It is twenty-two miles east-north-east of Damascus, and is supposed to be the ancient Adarisi.
