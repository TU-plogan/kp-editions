KENWYN, a parish in the county of Cornwall, within the hundred of Powder, 257 miles from London. It joins the town of Truro, and forms a kind of suburb to it. The inhabitants amounted in 1801 to 4017, in 1811 to 5000, in 1821 to 6221, and in 1831 to 8492.
