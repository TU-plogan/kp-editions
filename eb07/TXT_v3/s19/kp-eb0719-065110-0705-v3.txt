SAROS, in chronology, a period of 223 lunar months. The etymology of the word is said to be Chaldaean, signifying restitution or return of eclipses; that is, conjunctions of the sun and moon in nearly the same place of the ecliptic. The Saros was a cycle like that of Meto.
