FREEDOM, in general, the state or quality of being free.

Freedom of a Corporation, the right of enjoying all the privileges and immunities belonging to it.

Freedom of Conscience. See Toleration. Freedom of the WiU, that power or faculty of the mind by which it is capable of acting or not acting, choosing or rejecting, whatever it judges proper. See Metaphysics.
