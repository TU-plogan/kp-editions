SUGAT, a town of Asia Minor, in the pachalic of Bursa, supposed to be on the site of the ancient Tottarium. The houses arc generally built of mud and wood, two stories high, with projecting verandahs, and roofed with common red tile. It is remarkable for the tomb of Ali Osman, held in high respect by the natives.
