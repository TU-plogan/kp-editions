ATTORNEY at Law answers to the procurator or proctor of the civilians and canonists. He is one who is put in the place, stead, or room of another, to manage his matters of law.

Attorney-General, a great law-officer under the king, whose duty it is to exhibit informations, and prosecute for the crown, in matters criminal, and to file bills in the exchequer for any thing concerning the king in inheritance or profits.
