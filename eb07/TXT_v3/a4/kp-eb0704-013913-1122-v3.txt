ATTACOTTI, an ancient people of Britain, mentioned by Ammianus Marcellinus and St Jerome, as well as in the Notitia Imperii. They are represented as allies and confederates of the Scots and Picts, and therefore probably their neighbours; though their precise situation has not been determined by antiquaries.
