HESTON, a parish in the hundred of Islesworth, of the county of Middlesex, ten miles from London, the chief part of which consists of the town of Hounslow, through which the great roads to the western counties pass. The inhabitants amounted in 1801 to 1782, in 1811 to 2251, in 1821 to 2810, and in 1831 to 3407.
