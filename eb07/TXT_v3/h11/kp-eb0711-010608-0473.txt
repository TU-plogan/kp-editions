HAILSHAM, a market-town of the county of Sussex, in the rape of Pevensey, and hundred of Dill, fifty-seven miles from London, with a small market, which is held on Wednesday. The population amounted in 1801 to 897, in 1811 to 1029, in 1821 to 1278, and in 1831 to 1445.
