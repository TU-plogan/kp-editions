HARLING, East, a town of Norfolk, in the hundred [7:11:148]of Guilteross, ninety-three miles from London, with a market on Thursdays. The inhabitants amounted in 1801 to 674, in 1811 to 752, in 1821 to 867, and in 1831 to 1031.
