BRIXEN, a city in the Austrian province of Tyrol and circle of Pusterthal, at the confluence of the rivers Eisack and Rientz. It contains a cathedral, four other churches, and a nunnery for English ladies. It is situated in long. 12. 39. 30. E. and lat. 46. 40. N.
