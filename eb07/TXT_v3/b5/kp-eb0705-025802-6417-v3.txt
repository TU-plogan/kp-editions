BRIANSK, a circle in the Russian government of Orel, with a population of 68,540 persons. The chief place is the city of the same name, on the river Desna. It trades in iron, corn, hemp, flax, and oak-bark, and contains about 6000 inhabitants. Long. 34. 14. E. Lat. 53. 21. N.
