BRIGG, or Glandford Brigg, a market-town in the hundred of Yarborough and county of Lincoln, 153 miles from London, on the river Ancohne, which is navigable to the Humber. There is a large corn-market here on Thursday. The inhabitants amounted in 1801 to 1327, in 1811 to 1497, and in 1821 to 1674.
