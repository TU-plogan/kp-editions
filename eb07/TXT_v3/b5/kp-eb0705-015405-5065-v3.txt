BOUSSAC, an arrondissement of the department of the Creuse, in France, extending over 370 square miles, and comprehending four cantons and fifty-seven communes, with 32,439 inhabitants. The capital is a markettown of the same name, at the junction of the rivers Be-ron and Creuse. ,
