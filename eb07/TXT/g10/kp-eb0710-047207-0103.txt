GERMANTOWN, a post-town of North America, in the state of Pennsylvania, and county of Philadelphia. It is pleasantly situated about seven miles north of Philadelphia, the capital. Most of the houses are built in one continuous line of street, which is about two miles in length. This place possesses considerable manufactures, and a population of about 5000.
