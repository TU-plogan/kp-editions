GOONEE, a river of Hindustan, in the provinceor Sinde, which rises in the hills of Poollejar, and flowing [7:10:615]in a westerly direction, afterwards falls into the Indus, with which it forms a very large island called Majur. During the rainy season it is navigable for boats from Mandavie, a port of Cutch, to Hyderabad, the capital of Sinde.
