GOUDHURST, a town of the county of Kent, in the hundred of Marden, within the lathe of Scray, forty-four miles from London. It was once a place of some consequence in the woollen manufacture, but that has decayed. The church is a spacious, handsome building. The inhabitants amounted in 1821 to 2579. and in 1831 tn 2758.
