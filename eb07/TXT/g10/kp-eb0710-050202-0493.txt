GIAVENO, a city of Italy, in the kingdom of Sardinia and province of Susa. It stands on a hill, at whose base the river Sangone flows. It has the remains of an ancient wall, and contains a college. The inhabitants amount to 8300, who are employed in silk manufactures and in tanneries, and make some very good wine.
