GORZ, a fortified city of the Austrian province of Trieste, on the river Isonzo, containing, besides a cathedral and four other churches, 707 houses, with 9050 inhabitants, who are engaged in silk manufactories, in making leather, and bleaching wax. Near to it is Monte Santo, celebrated for the goodness of its wine. Long. 13. 23. E. Lat. 45. 57. N.
