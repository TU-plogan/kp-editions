GALACTOPHAGI, and Galactopotae, in Antiquity, persons who lived wholly on milk, without corn or the use of any other food. The words are compounded of yαλα, yaaaxro{, milk ∕ φaγut, to eat ; and τornj, from ⅞itiκs, I drink.
