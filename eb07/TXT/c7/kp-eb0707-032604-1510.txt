CORIGLIANO, a city of Italy, in the Neapolitan province of Otranto, containing 4000 inhabitants.

Corigliano, a city of Italy, in the Neapolitan province of Calabria Citeriore. It lies in the midst of a very extensive olive wood, from which oil is made, the trade in which furnishes subsistence to the 6000 inhabitants.
