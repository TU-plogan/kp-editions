CUSP (cw,Ïˆzs) properly denotes the point of a spear or sword; but it is used in astronomy to express the points or horns of the moon, or any other luminary.

Cusp, in Astrology, is used for the first point of each of the twelve houses, in a figure or scheme of the heavens.
