CONGOON, a town of Laristan, in Persia, situated on the northern shore of the Persian Gulf. It contains six or seven thousand inhabitants, and has an excellent roadstead, where a frigate may ride in safety in the most tempestuous weather. Good water and firewood, which are both scarce in the gulf, may be procured at this place. Long. 52. 5. E. Lat. 27. 50. N.
