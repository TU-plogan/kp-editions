COXACHIE, or Cocksackie, a township of the United States, in Greene county, New York. This place has been principally built since 1800; and it now enjoys a considerable and still increasing trade. It is distant twenty-two miles south of Albany, and eight north of the Hudson. The population amounts to about 5000.
