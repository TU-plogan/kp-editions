CONTRA-HARMONIC Proportion, is that relation of three terms, in which the difference of the first and second is to the difference of the second and third as the third is to the first. Thus, for instance, 3, 5, and G, are numbers contra-harmonically proportional; for 2: 1 :: G: 3.
