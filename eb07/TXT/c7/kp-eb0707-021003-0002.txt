CONEGLI ANO, a city of Italy, in the delegation of Treviso, of the Austrian kingdom of Venetian Lombardy. It is situated on the river Mutego, at the foot of a mountain. It contains 4183 inhabitants, industriously employed in silk and cloth manufacture. Long. 12. 14. E. Lat. 45. 52. N.
