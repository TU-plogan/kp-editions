COOSERAH, a town of Hindustan, in the province of Bahar. Itformerly possessed one of those strong forts which are so common, and which enabled the Zemindars to def; the authority of government. It is forty-eight miles S.E from Patna. Long. 85. 47. E. Lat. 28. 6. N.
