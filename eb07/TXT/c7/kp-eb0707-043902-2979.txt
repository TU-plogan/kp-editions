COZIQUIRIACHI, or Cosxguiriachi, Santa Rosa de, a town of Mexico in the intendancy of Durango, surrounded with silver mines. The population of this place amounts to nearly 11,000. It is distant twenty-four leagues south-west of Chihuahua.
