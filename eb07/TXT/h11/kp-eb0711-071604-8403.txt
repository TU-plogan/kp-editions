HUBNER, John, a learned geographer of Germany, who taught geography at Leipsic and Hamburg with extraordinary reputation; and died at Hamburg in 1732, aged sixty-three. His principal work is a Geographical Treatise, printed at Basil in 1746, in six vols. 12mo.
