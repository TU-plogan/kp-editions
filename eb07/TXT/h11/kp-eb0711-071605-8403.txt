HUCKNALL-TORKARD, a town in the hundred of Broxtow, in the county of Nottingham, 130 miles from London, where there are manufactures of hosiery. The inhabitants amounted in 1801 to 1497, in 1811 to 1793, in 1821 to 1940, and in 1831 to 2200.
