HEILSBERG, a city, the capital of a circle of the same name, in the province of East Prussia, situated on the river Alle. It is the residence of the Catholic bishop of Ermeland, contains two Catholic and one Lutheran church, with 348 houses, and 2850 inhabitants. Long. 20. 37. 50 E. Lat. 54. 3. 50. N.
