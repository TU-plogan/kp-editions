HUNFELD, a town of the province of Fulda, in Hesse-Cassel, the capital of a bailiwick of the same name, which contains one town and thirty-two villages, with 7633 inhabitants. The town is situated on the river Haun, and has some considerable bleacheries, with 1730 inhabitants.
