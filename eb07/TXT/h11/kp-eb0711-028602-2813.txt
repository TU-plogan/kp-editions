HESBIN, a city of France, in the arrondissement of Montreuil, and department of Calais. It stands at the junction of the river Canche with the Ternaise, in a marshy situation, and is strongly fortified. It contains 938 houses, and 4160 inhabitants, who produce hosiery and cotton goods, and have some twist mills. Long. 2. 3. E. Lat. 50. 22. N.
