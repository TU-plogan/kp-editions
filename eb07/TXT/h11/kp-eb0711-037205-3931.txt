HINDON, a borough-town of the county of Wilts, formerly a part of the pari >h of East Knowle, in the hundred of Downton. It is ninety-six miles from London, has a small market on Thursday, and its population amounted in 1801 to 793, in 1811 to 781, in 1821 to 830, and in 1831 to 932.
