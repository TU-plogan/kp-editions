HARPISCHORD, the most harmonious of all the musical instruments of the stringed kind. It is played on after the manner of the organ, and is furnished with one set, sometimes with two sets of keys. The touching or striking of these keys moves a kind of little jacks, which also move a double row of chords or strings of brass or iron, stretched oyer four bridges on the tabic of the instrument.
