HEILIGENSTADT, a town of the province of Lower Ens, in Austria, a few miles distant from Vienna. It contains some manufactories of paper, silks, cotton twist, and oil refining. There is an hospital or school for the daughters of the officers of the army. The inhabitants are 2650.

Heiligenstadt, a city, the capital of the circle of the same name, in the province of Saxony, in Prussia. It stands at the junction of the rivers Geislade and Leine. It is surrounded with walls, contains four churches, 560 houses, and 4530 inhabitants, who are employed in the woollen trade, and in distilleries.
