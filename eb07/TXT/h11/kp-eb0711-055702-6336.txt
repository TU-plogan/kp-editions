HOOLAIVA, or Holaiva, one of the Hapace islands, in the South Pacific Ocean, which was found in a state of nature when visited by Captain Cook in 1777. It is joined to Lefooga by a reef that is dry at low water; and is thirty miles north-north-east of Annamooka. Long. 185. 36. E. Lat. 19. 52. N.
