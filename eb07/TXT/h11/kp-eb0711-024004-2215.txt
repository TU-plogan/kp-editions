HEPTAGONAL Numbers, in Arithmetic, a sort of polygonal numbers, in which the difference of the terms of the corresponding arithmetical progression is 5. One of the properties of these numbers is, that if they be multiplied by 40, and 9 be added to the product, the sum will be a square number.
