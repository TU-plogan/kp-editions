MARTOCK, a town of Somersetshire, in the hundred of the same name, 130 miles from London. It is in a rich level district, through which runs the river Ivel. The church is a large and elegant structure. In the market-place is a fluted column after the model of the celebrated pillar of Trajan. The population amounted in 1801 to 2102, in 1811 to 2356, in 1821 to 2560, and in Ä°831 to 2841.
