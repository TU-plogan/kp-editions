MESTRE, a market town, the chief place of a district in the delegation of Venice, in Italy. It stands on the Mar-cenigo, contains many small houses, inhabited by 5320 persons, who are mostly carriers or gondoliers. It is in lat. 45. 29. 6. N. Long. 11. 44.40. E.
