MASSILIA, in Ancient Geography, a town of Gallia Narbonensis, peopled by a colony of 1 hoccans, from Pho- aca, a city of Ionia, and universally celebrated, not only or its port, commerce, and strength, but especially for the politeness and learning of its inhabitants. It is now Marseilles, a maritime city of Provence.
