MARLY, a town of the department of the Seine and Oise, and arrondissement of Versailles, in France. It is near the left bank of the Seine, and celebrated for its machinery, by which Versailles is supplied with water. A most splendid castle was built here by Louis XIV., which has been converted into a cloth manufactory. It contains 320 houses and 1250 inhabitants.
