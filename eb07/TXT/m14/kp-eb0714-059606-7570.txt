MESCHTSCHOWSK, a circle of the Russian province of Kaluga, extending over 684 square miles, and containing 78,600 inhabitants. The capital is a town of the same name, 648 miles from St. Petersburg, on the river Tureja, with 360 houses, and 1830 inhabitants. Lat. 44. 22. N. Long. 34. 55. E.
