MENIN, a city of the Netherlands, in the province of West Flanders, and arrondisement of Courtray. It is situ ated on the river Lys, which is the boundary towards France. It is strongly fortified, and contains 4560 inhabitants, who manufacture linen, woollen, and cotton goods, lace, paper, [7:14:560]leather, and snuff, and carry on a considerable trade in com, cattle, and flax.
