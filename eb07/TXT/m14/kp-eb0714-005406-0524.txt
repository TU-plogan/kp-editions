MALDA, a town of Hindustan, in the province of Bengal and district of Rajemahal, situated on a river which communicates with the Ganges. It is a place of great trade, and produces much silk. It is fifty-six miles north by west from Moorshedabad. Long. 88. 4. E. Lat. 25. 3. N.
