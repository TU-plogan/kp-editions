MEMMINGEN, a city of Bavaria, in the circle of the Upper Danube, and the capital of the courts of law of the several bailiwicks. It is situated in a pleasant district, on the river Ach, and contains three Lutheran and three Catholic churches, 1150 houses, and 6640 inhabitants. It is an old town, surrounded with walls, and some large antique public buildings.
