MAZEIRA, a considerable island near the eastern coast of Arabia, about sixty miles long and eight broad. The passage between it and the mainland admits large vessels. There is a village on the eastern shore, which is seldom visited by Europeans. Long, of the north-east part 59. 40. E. Lat. 20. 35. N.
