MERIDEN, a town of the county of Warwick, situated in the hundred of Hemlingford, and ninety-seven miles from London. It is a great posting place, and near to it is the fine house and park of the Earls of Aylesford. The inhabitants were in 1801, 821; in 1811, 817; in 1821, 927; and in 1831, 892.
