MARASDIN, a city of the circle of the same name, in the Austrian province of Croatia. It stands on the river Drave, is the seat of the provincial boards of civil and criminal law, contains five Catholic churches, four monasteries, and a nunnery, with 749 houses, and 4436 inhabitants. It has a considerable commercial intercourse with Turkey. Long. 23. 22. 10. E. Lat. 46. 18. 18. N.
