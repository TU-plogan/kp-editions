MARCHERS, or Lords-Marchers, were those noblemen who lived on the marches of Wales or Scotland, and who, in times past, according to Camden, ⅛ιad their laws and jus vitoe necisque, like petty kings. In old records the lords marchers of Wales were styled Marchianes de Marchia Walliae.
