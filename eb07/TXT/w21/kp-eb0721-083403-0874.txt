WEDMORE, a town and parish of the county of Somerset, in the hundred of Bempstone, 126 miles from London and five from Axbridge. It had formerly a market, which has of late years ceased to be attended. The population of the parish amounted in 1821 to 3097, and in 1831 to 3557.
