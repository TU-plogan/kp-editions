WADEY Abassi, a small river of Arabia, which falls into the Red Sea ten miles south-south-east of Hodeida.

Wadey el Arkik, a small river of Arabia, which waters the city of Medina.

Wadey el Kebir, a small river in Arabia, which in rainy seasons reaches the Red Sea near Mocha.

Wadey Elmahan, a small river of Arabia, which loses itself in the sands, unless in the wet season, when it reaches the Red Sea twenty-five miles south-south-east of Hodeida.

Wadey Faran, a small river of Arabia, which falls into the Red Sea twenty-five miles north-west of Tor.

Wadey Zebid, a river of Arabia, which passes by Zebid, and at a particular season overflows and fertilizes its banks. It finally loses itself in the sands.
