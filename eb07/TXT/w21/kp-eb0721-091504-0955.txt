WOLTSCHANSK, a circle of the Russian government of Ukraine. It extends in north latitude from 49° 48’ to 50° 32', and in east longitude from 36° 22' to 37° 38'. It comprehends one city and 110 towns, besides villages, with 88,400 inhabitants, in a space of 1254 square miles. It has many small rivers, which empty their waters into the Donez. The soil is generally fertile and the climate temperate. The capital is a city of the same name as the circle, and as the river on which it is built. It contains 410 houses, two churches, and 2850 inhabitants. It is 990 miles from St Petersburg. Long. 37. 8. E. Lat. 50. 25. N.
