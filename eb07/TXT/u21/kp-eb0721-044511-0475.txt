UGOCSA, a circle of the Austrian kingdom of Hungary, in the province of the Hither Theiss. It extends over 480 square miles, one half of which is covered with woods. It comprehends three market-towns and sixty-three villages, with 5390 houses, and 36,900 inhabitants, [7:21:446]mostly Catholics, with 2000 Protestants and 1500 Jews. The chief town is Nagyszollos, with 2160 inhabitants, whose chief trade is in wine. Long. 22. 44 34. E. Lat. 48. 7. 10. N.
