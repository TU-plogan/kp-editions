PAOTING, a city of China, of the first rank, in the province of Pe-che-lee. It is the residence of the viceroy, and ranks next to Pekin amongst the cities of the province. It is seventy-seven miles south-south-west of Pekin. Long. 115. 14. E. Lat. 38. 54. N.
