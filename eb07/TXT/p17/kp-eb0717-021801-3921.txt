PENELLA, a town of the kingdom of Portugal, in the province of Beira, and corregimiento of Cambra. It is situated upon an elevated spot, and is defended by two castles. It contains two churches, an hospital, and a poorhouse, 746 dwellings, and 3760 inhabitants.
