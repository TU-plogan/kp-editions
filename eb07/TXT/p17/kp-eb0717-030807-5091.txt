PESCIA, a city of the duchy of Tuscany, in Italy, the capital of a district, in the province of Florence, of its own name. It is situated on the river Pescia, is the see of a bishop, and has a cathedral and two other churches, with [7:17:309]4200 inhabitants, who subsist chiefly by spinning and weaving silk, and making paper and pottery wares.
