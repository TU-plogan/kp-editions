PASSAGE Island, a small island amongst the Philippines, about twenty miles east from the island of Leyta. Long. 125. 27. E. Lat. 10. 44. N.

Passage Isle, a small island on the northern coast of Sumatra, which here forms a deep bay, where there are several Malay ports. Long. 97. 55. E. Lat. 2. 31. N.
