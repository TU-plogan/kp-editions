PELING, an island in the Yellow Sea, near the coast of Corea, ten miles in length and four in breadth. Long. 124. 28. E. Lat. 38. 24. N.

Peling Isle, an island in the Eastern Seas, near the east coast of the island of Celebes, between the 123d and 124th degrees of east longitude. It is fifty miles in length by fifteen in breadth, and is surrounded by many smaller isles, with numerous rocks and shoals. Long. 124. 28. E. Lat. 38. 24. N.
