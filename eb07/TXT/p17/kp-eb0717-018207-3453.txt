PELUSSIN, a town of France, in the department of the Loire, and arrondissement of St Etienne. It has some considerable manufactories of silk; and the neighbourhood is remarkable for the great quantity of chestnuts which are collected annually for exportation. It contains about 460 houses, with 3350 inhabitants.
