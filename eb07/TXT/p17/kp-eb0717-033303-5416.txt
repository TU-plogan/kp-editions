PETSCHANOI, a fortress of Asiatic Russia, in the government of Kolivan, 188 miles west-south-west of Kolivan. Also a cape on the northern coast of Asiatic Russia, in the Frozen Sea. Long. 165. 14. E. Lat. 75. 25. N.
