OTTAJANO, a city of Italy, in the province Terra di Lavoro, in the kingdom of Naples. It is a large place, in a district producing considerable quantities of silk and olive oil, and abundant crops of corn. It contains three parish churches, and, including the adjacent village, 14,200 inhabitants.
