ORTONE a Mare, a city of Italy, on the sea-shore, in the province of Abbruzzo Citeriore, of the kingdom of Naples. It is situated near to Cape Aquabella, is surrounded with walls, is the see of a bishop, and contains 5680 inhabitants. It has no haven, but a good roadstead.
