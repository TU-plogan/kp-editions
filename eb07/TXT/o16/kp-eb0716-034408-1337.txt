OPARO, an island in the Pacific Ocean, discovered by Captain Vancouver in 1791. It consists of a cluster of high craggy mountains. The natives are estimated at 1500, who appeared to be well fed, and to have open and cheerful countenances. Long. 215. 58. E. Lat. 27. 36. S.
