OUTING, a city of China, of the first rank, in the province of Yunan, situated in a fertile country, near a range of very rugged mountains, which yield abundance of musk. It is exposed to the inroads of the mountaineers, for which reason a considerable garrison is maintained in the place. Long. 102. 6. E. Lat. 25. 53. N.
