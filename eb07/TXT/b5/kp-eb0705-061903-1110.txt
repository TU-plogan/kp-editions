BUA, an island in the Adriatic Sea, belonging to the province of Spalatro, in the Austrian kingdom of Dalmatia. It is situated on the coast, opposite to Trau; is rich in wine, oil, and fruit; and contains six populous villages. There is in it a remarkable well of asphaltum. Long. 14. 10. E. Lat. 43. 8. N.
