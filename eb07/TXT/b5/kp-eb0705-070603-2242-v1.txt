BUNGAY; a market-town of the hundred ofWangford, in the county of Suffolk, 107 miles from London, on the river Waveney, which divides it from the county of Norfolk, and is navigable to the sea at Yarmouth. It is a well-built town, comprising two parishes with their churches, and the ruins of an ancient monastery and of acastle. There is but little trade except on the Thursdayâ€™s market.
