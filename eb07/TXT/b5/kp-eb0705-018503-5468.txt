BRASIDA, an anniversary solemnity at Sparta, in memory of Brasidas, a Lacedemonian captain, famous for his achievements at Methone, Pylos, and Amphipolis. It was celebrated with sacrifices and games, in which none were permitted to attend but free-born Spartans. Whosoever neglected to be present at the solemnity was fined.
