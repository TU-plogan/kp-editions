BREDSTEDT, a town, the capital of a bailiwick, containing one town and nine parishes, in the duchy of Sleswick and kingdom of Denmark. It stands on the seashore, and contains 309 houses, with 1590 inhabitants. Long. 8. 53. E. Lat. 54. 38. N.
