BRAHILOW, or in Turkish Ibrainl, a city on the northern bank of the Danube, in the province of Wal-lachia, in European Turkey. It has a strong citadel to defend the passage over the river, which is here divided into several branches. The trade of the place is considerable, both in corn and in preserved sturgeons. It is said to contain, besides the garrison, 12,500 inhabitants. Long. 27. 49. E. Lat. 44. 56. N.
