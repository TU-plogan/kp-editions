BRIGNOLES, an arrondissement of the department of the Var, in France. Its extent is 492 square miles, and it contains eight cantons, twenty-six communes, and 66,184 inhabitants. The chief place, of the same name, is situated on the river Salme, in a mountainous district, containing 5301 souls. Long. 6. 50. E. Lat. 43. 24. N.
