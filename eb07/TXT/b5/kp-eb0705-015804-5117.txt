BOXHORNIUS, Marc Zuerius, a learned critic, born at Bergen-op-Zoom in 1612, was professor of eloquence at Leyden, and at length of politics and history in the room of Heinsius. He published, 1. Theatrum urbium Hollan-dice, Amsterdam, 1632; 2. Scriptores historice Augustee, cum notis, Leyden, 1632; 3. Poetee satyrici minores, cum commentis, 1632; 4. Notes on Justin, Tacitus; and a great number of other works. He died in 1653, aged forty-one.
