SALLAWATTY, one of the Papuan or Oriental Negro Isles, separated from the island of Papua or New Guinea by a narrow strait. It is thirty miles in length by twenty-five in average breadth, and produces abundance of sago of an excellent quality. It is situated about the 13lst degree of east longitude.
