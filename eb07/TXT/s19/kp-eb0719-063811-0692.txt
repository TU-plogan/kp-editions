SANDEC, a circle in the Austrian kingdom of Gallicia, extending over 1147 square miles. It contains eight cities, five market-towns, and 386 villages, with 198,750 inhabitants. The capital is the city of New Sandec, situated on the river Dunajec, and contains 490 houses, with 3840 inhabitants.
