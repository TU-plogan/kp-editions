PALATSHA, a village of Anatolia, in Asia Minor, situated near the coast of the Mediterranean. It is supposed by some to be the ancient Miletus, whilst others believe the ruins to be those of Myus, an ancient town, ultimately incorporated with Miletus. Long. 27.12. E. Lat. 37.31. N.
