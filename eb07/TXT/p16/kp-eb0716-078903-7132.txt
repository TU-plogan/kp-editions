PANGOOTARRAN, one of the Sooloo Archipelago Isles, in the Eastern Seas, about ten miles in length by four in average breadth. It is one entire bed of coral rock, with scarcely any appearance of soil on it. It abounds, however, in cocoa-nut trees, and has also plenty of cattle, goats, and fowls. Long. 120. 30. E. Lat. 6. 9.
