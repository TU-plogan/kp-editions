PANHA, a village of Hindustan, tributary to the Nepaul rajahs, in the province of Serinagur. It contains forty or fifty huts. Long. 79. 10. E. Lat. 30. 18. N. About six miles to the south of this place are the copper mines of Dhanpoor, and eight miles to the north those of Nagpoor.
