NESTON, a town of the county of Chester, in the hundred of Wirral, near Parkgate, ten miles from the city of Chester, and 190 miles from London. The parish is large, and contains no fewer than seven other townships, Ï‡ Great Neston had, in 1801, 1486 inhabitants; in 1811, 1332; in 1821, 1418; and in 1831, 1633.
