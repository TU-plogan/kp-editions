NEUSOL, a city in the circle of Soler, in the province of the Lower Danube, in Hungary. It is situated on the river Gran, and is the place of assembly of the states of the comitat, and of the several boards of the government. It contains Catholic and Lutheran churches, a castle, 800 houses, and 10,069 inhabitants. There are also seminaries for the education of Catholics and Protestants; and the trade in iron and copper articles is considerable. Near to it good wine is produced, and there is an extensive royal manufactory of warlike arms. Long. 19. 4. 25. E. Lat. 48. 45. N.
