NURR AH, a town of Hindustan, in the province of Gundwana, tributary to the Nagpoor rajah, and possessed by native Goand chiefs. It is seventy-eight miles south by east from Ruttunpoor. Long. 82. 45. E. Lat. 21. 2. N.
