NECROPOLIS, a suburb of Alexandria, in Egypt. It signifies the City of the Dead, and was the place where temples, gardens, and superb mausoleums were erected. Here Cleopatra is said to have applied the asp to her breast, to prevent her being led in triumph by Augustus, who endeavoured to save her.
