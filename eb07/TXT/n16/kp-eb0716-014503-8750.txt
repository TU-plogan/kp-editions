NEUGRADISKA, a town of Hungary, in the Sclavonian military frontier province, the capital of the circle of Gradiska, which extends over 668 square miles, and has a population of 59,124 persons, from whom the regiment of its name is supplied with recruits. The town is fortified, and contains a Catholic and a Greek church, with 209 houses and 1349 inhabitants. Long. 27. 19. 27. E. Lat. 45. 27. N.
