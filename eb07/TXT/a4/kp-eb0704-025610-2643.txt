AVSTA, a mining village in the barony of Rasgard and province of Storakopparber (formerly Dalcarleia), in Sweden. It suffered by fire in 1803, but has since been rebuilt and increased. It contains 115 houses and 600 inhabitants, all employed in the various mineral products of the vicinity. There is a mint for coining copper money.
