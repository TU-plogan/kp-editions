PORDENONE, a city of Austrian Italy, in the province of Milan, and delegation of Bclluno. It stands on the river Roncello, and contains 650 houses, with 4230 inhabitants, who are chiefly employed in making linen goods and paper. Lat. 45. 56. 42. Long. 12. 34. 54. E.
