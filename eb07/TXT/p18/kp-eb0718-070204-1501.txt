PSALTERIUM, Greek ∙ψαλrjjp∕oι⅛ formerly called ma-gadis, according to Athenaeus, an ancient musical instrument, which some writers assert to have been the same as the ancient Hebrew nebel. The more modern psaltery being no longer in use, it is unnecessary to describe it.
