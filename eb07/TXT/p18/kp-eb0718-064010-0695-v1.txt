PROCLUS, surnamed Diadocus, a Greek philosopher and mathematician,was born in Lycia, and lived about the year 500. He was the disciple of Syrianus; and the friend of the Emperor Anastasius. It is said, that when Vitalian laid siege to Constantinople, Proclus burned his ships with large brazen specula. This philosopher was a Pagan, and wrote against the Christian religion.
