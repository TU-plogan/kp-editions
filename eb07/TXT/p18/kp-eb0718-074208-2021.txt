PUTIWL, a city of Russia, in the government of Kursk, the capital of a circle of the same name. It stands at the junction of the Putiwl with'the Gem, and is a very ancient place, surrounded with pallisades. It contains nineteen churches, 1276 houses, and 10,500 inhabitants, who are occupied in refining saltpetre, making vitriol, and in other branches of internal trade. It is 890 miles from St Petersburg. Long. 33. 55. E. Lat. 52. 21. N.
