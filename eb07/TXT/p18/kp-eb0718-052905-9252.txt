PRETEXT, a colour or motive, whether real or feigned, for doing something.

Toga PRETEXTA, amongst the ancient Romans, a long white gown, with a border of purple round the edges. It was worn by children of quality till the age of puberty; that is, by the boys till the age of seventeen, tvhen they changed it for the toga virilis, and by the girls till marriage.
