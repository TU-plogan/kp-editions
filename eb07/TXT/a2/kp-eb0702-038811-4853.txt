ALCORA, a town of Spain, in the province of Valencia. It is situated on a small river that runs into the Mijares, and is principally to be noted for its potteries, in which earthenware of various kinds and china are extensively manufactured. It contains 2400 inhabitants, and is situated in lat. 42. 2. N.
