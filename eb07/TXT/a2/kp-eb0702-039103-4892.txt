ALCOY, a Spanish city on the sea-coast, in the province of Valencia. It has one parish church, three monasteries, and 14,600 inhabitants. It has a manufactory for [7:2:392]fine cloth, a soap-boiling house, and a very large manufactory for paper, which employs 48 mills.
