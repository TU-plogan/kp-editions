ABBOTS-BROMLEY, a town in Staffordshire. After the dissolution of the monasteries, it was given to Lord Paget; and has since been called Paget’s Bromley. But it retains its old name in the king’s books, and with regard to the fairs. Population in 1801, 808; in 1811, 1019; and in 1821, 1533.
