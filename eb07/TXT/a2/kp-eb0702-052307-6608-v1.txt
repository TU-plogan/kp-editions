It contains two cities, 19 large parishes, and 21,635 inhabitants. The district is full of small lakes and extensive woods. The soil is bad, and yields little more than rye, oats, potatoes, and buckwheat, with some flax. The most extensive of the lakes are the Dadai and the Wadang. It is watered by [7:2:524] the rivers Alle, Pisa, and some smaller streams. The capital is a city on the Alle, of the same name.
