ALMARAZ, a town of Spain, in the province of Old Castile. It is on the north bank of the Tagus, where the road from Badajos divides into two branches, the one proceeding towards Madrid, the other towards Plasencia and Ciudad Rodrigo. The destruction of the bridge over the Tagus was one of the most brilliant actions of the late war.
