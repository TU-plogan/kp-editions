ALHUMECAS, an island belonging to Spain, on the coast of Africa, near Ceuta. There is a town on it, near which is good anchorage for small vessels. The place is used as a receptacle for criminals; and there are within it three capacious cisterns for preserving fresh water. Long. 3. 12. W. Lat. 35. 16. 30. N.
