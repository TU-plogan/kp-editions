ALFARO, a town of Spain, on the confluence of Alhama with the Ebro, in the province of Soria. It is surrounded with walls, and has four gates, one church, and four monasteries, and 4800 inhabitants. It contains four houses for soap-boiling, which produce 250 cwts.; three tanneries, and 28 looms for weaving linen.
