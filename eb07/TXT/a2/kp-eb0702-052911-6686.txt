ALMADIE, a kind of canoe or small vessel, about four fathoms long, commonly made of bark, and used by the negroes of Africa.

Almadie is also the name of a kind of long boats, fitted out at Calicut, which are 80 feet in length and six or seven in breadth. They are exceedingly swift, and are otherwise called cathuri.
