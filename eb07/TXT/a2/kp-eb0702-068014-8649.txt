ANACLETERIA, in Antiquity, a solemn festival celebrated by the ancients when their kings or princes came of age, and assumed the reins of government. It is so. called, because proclamation being made of this event to the people, they went to salute their prince during the anacleteria, and to congratulate him upon his new dignity.
