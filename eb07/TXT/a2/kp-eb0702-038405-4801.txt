ALBIS, in Ancient Geography, now the Elbe, which divided ancient Germany in the middle, and was the boundary of this country, so far as it was known to the Romans; all beyond they owned to be uncertain, no Roman except Drusus and Tiberius having penetrated so far as the Elbe.
