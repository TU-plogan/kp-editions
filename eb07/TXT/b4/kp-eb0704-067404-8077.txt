BLANCHING, the art or method of making any thing white. Sec Bleaching. Blanching, in coinage, the operation performed on the planchets, or pieces of silver, to give them the requisite lustre and brightness.

Blanching, among gardeners, an operation by which certain salads and roots are rendered whiter than they would otherwise be. See Horticulture.
