BLAUBEUERN, a bailiwick in the circle of the Danube and kingdom of Wirtemburg, extending over 132 square miles, and comprehending one city, of the same name, with 1860 inhabitants, one market-town, and fifty-six villages, with a population of 15,646 persons. The climate is cold, and the soil rather unproductive.
