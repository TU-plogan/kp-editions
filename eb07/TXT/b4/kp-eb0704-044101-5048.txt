BATHERI, a village of Northern Hindostan, situated among the mountains of SerinÎµgur. It has a sacred temple, and is situated 300 feet above the bed of the Ganges. On the opposite side of the river is an extraordinary cascade, which issues from the summit of the mountain. Long. 78. 30. E. Lat. 30. 49. N.
