BANK, in Political Economy, an establishment for keeping money secure, and for dealing in money by discounting bills and otherwise. The history of banks will be given under the article Money. That class of banks whose object is to encourage habits of saving among labourers, called banks for savings, will be treated under the head of Savings Banks.
