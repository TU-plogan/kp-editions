BOAD, a town of Hindostan, in the province of Orissa, on the south side of the river Mahanuddy. It is possessed by an independent zemindar; and, like all the neighbouring towns and villages, it is fenced with bamboos, [7:4:721]to protect the inhabitants and their cattle from the wild beasts. It is 55 miles south-south-east of Sumbulpoor. , Long. 84. 18. E. Lat. 20. 50. N.
