BEVERAGE, in a general sense, signifies a drink; and hence nectar is said to be the beverage of the gods. In writers of the middle ages, beverage, beveragium, or bibe-rÎ±giurn, denotes money given to an artificer or other person, to drink, over and above his hire or wages.
