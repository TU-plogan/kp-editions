BEREZOVSKOI, a town and fortress of Asiatic Russia, in the government of Orenburg, situated on the river Oural. It has near it four gold mines from five to twenty fathoms deep, from which are extracted annually 100 pounds of gold dust. It is 140 miles E. N. E. of Orenburg.
