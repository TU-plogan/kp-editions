BOLTING, a term of art used in our inns of court, by which is meant a private arguing of cases.

Bolting, or Boulting, the act of separating the flour from the bran by means of a sieve or bolter.

Bolting- Cloth, or Bolster-cloth, sometimes also called Boulting-cloth, denotes a linen or hair-cloth for sifting meal or flour.
