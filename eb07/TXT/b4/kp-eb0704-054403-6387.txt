BELGOROD, a circle in the Russian government of Kursk, which contains a population of 32,300 persons. The chief place is the city of the same name, on the river Do-ncz. It is the seat of an archbishop, and has considerable linen and other manufactures, five churches, 1695 houses, and about 9000 inhabitants. Long. 35.56. E. Lat. 50. 55. N.
