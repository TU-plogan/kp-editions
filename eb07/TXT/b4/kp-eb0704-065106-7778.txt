BISCHOFSHEIM, a city, the chief of a bailiwick of the same name, in the duchy of Baden, on the river Tauber, containing 2000 inhabitants. The bailiwick comprehends three cities or towns, eighteen villages and hamlets, an d 16,200 inhabitants.
