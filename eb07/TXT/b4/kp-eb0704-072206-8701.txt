BOBILEE, a town of Hindostan, in the northern circars. The Rangaroo of Bobilee was in 1757 one of the polygars of the first rank in this country; but being engaged in deadly enmity with a neighbouring polygar, who prevailed on the French commander Μ. Bussy to espouse his cause, he was attacked in his fort, the last retreat of every polygar when he is hard pressed; and seeing no hope of successful resistance, he put to death his whole family, and, along with his remaining troops, perished in defence of the fort. Long. 83. 28. Ľ. Lat. 18. 27. N.

BOCA-Chica, the strait or entrance into the harbour of Carthagena in South America. It is defended by several forts belonging to the Spaniards, all of which were taken by the English in 1741; but they were nevertheless obliged to raise the siege of Carthagena in a short time after.

BocA-del-Drago, a strait so called, between the island of Trinidad and Andalusia, in the province of Terra Firma in South America.
