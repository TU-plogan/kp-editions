BAUDRAND, Michael Anthony, a geographer, born at Paris on the 18th July 1633. He travelled into several countries, and then applied himself to the revisal of Ferrarius’s Geographical Dictionary, which he enlarged to the extent of one half. He wrote, 1. Notes to Papirius Masso’s Description of the Rivers of France; 2. A Geographical and Historical Dictionary; 3. Christian Geography, or an Account of the Archbishoprics and Bishoprics of the whole world; and constructed several maps. He died at Paris on the 29th of May 1700.
