BOGUTSCHAT, a circle in the Russian government of Woronesh, and a district productive of corn and cattle. The chief place, of the same name, stands at the junction of the Boguschara with the Don, and contains less than 1000 inhabitants. Long. 40. 35. E. Lat. 50. 5. N.
