BAHLINGEN, a bailiwick in the circle of Schwartz-wald, or the Black Forest, in the kingdom of Wirtemberg. It extends over 132 square miles, and comprehends two cities, one market-town, and thirty-five villages, with 28,164 inhabitants. Its agriculture is little productive, but manufactures of various kinds are more advanced. The city, which gives name to the bailiwick, is on the Eynach, and contains 3220 inhabitants. Long. 8.45. 52. E. Eat. 48. 16. 0. N.
