BALLET, Balet, or Balletto, a kind of dramatic poem, representing some fabulous action or subject divided into several entries; wherein several persons appear, and recite things under the name of some deity, or other illustrious character. .

Ballet also denotes a theatrical action, represented by dancing guided by music.
