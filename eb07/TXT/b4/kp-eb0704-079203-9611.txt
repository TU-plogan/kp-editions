BOONDEE, a town of Hindostan, in the province of Ajmeer, situated on the southern declivity of a long range of hills, which run nearly from east to west. It is the residence of a rajah, and is tributary to the Mahrattas. The palace of the rajah is fortified, and is a massive building of stone, situated on the brow of a hill Long. 75. 35. E. Lat. 25. 26. N.
