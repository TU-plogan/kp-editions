BOGORODIZK, a circle of the Russian government of Tula, comprehending one city, two market-towns, and 166 villages, with 125,000 inhabitants. The chief place, a city of the same name, on the river Lesnaja, has about 2800 inhabitants. Long. 37. 5. E. Lat. 53. 45. N.
