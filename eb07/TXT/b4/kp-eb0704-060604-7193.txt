BEUTELSPACH, a town in the circle of Jaxt, of the kingdom of Wirtemburg. It stands on the river Beutel, and is remarkable as containing the ancient monastery and church, in which are buried the old family of Wirtemburg. It contains 1789 inhabitants, whose labour is chiefly applied to produce wine, which is highly esteemed.
