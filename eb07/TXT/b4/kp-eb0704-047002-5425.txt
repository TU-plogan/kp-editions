BAZZANO, a city of Italy, in the papal delegation of Bologna, on the canal of St Giovanni. It contains about 3450 inhabitants.

BEACHY-Head, a promontory on the coast of Sussex between Hastings and Shoreham, where the French defeated the English and Dutch fleet in 1690.
