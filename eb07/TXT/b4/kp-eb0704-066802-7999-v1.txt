BLAEU, William, a famous printer of Amsterdam, as well as a disciple and friend of Tycho Brahe. His Atlas, his Treatises of the Globes, Astronomical Institutions, and other works, together with his fine impressions, have secured his name from oblivion. He died in 1638.
