BELLASPOOR, a town of Hindostan, in the province of Delhi, situated on the east bank of the Sutlege river, which is here 100 yards broad when the water is lowest. The houses are well and regularly built, of stone and mortar; and the streets are all paved, though roughly. It is the residence of the rany or female ruler of the Cor-lour territory. Long. 76. 21. E. Lat 31. 35. N.
