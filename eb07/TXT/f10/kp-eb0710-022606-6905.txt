FRAUENBURĠ, a city of the province of East Prussia, in the government of Königsberg, on the Frische Haff, at the foot of a hill on which stands the cathedral of which Copernicus was a canon, and where he was buried in 1545. It contains 195 houses, and 1720 inhabitants. Long. 19. 49. 20. E. Lat. 54. 21. 54. N.
