FULMINATION, in Chemistry. Many chemical combinations are accompanied by a loud report. This is termed fulmination, or more commonly detonation.

Fulmination, in the Romish canon law, is a sentence of a bishop, official, or other ecclesiastic appointed by the pope, by which it is decreed that some bull sent from the pope shall be executed.
