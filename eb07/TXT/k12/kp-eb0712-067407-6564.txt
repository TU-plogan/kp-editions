KANGELANG Isle, an island of a very irregular shape, and surrounded by a cluster of smaller ones, with innumerable shoals. The principal island is twenty-five miles long by eight in average breadth. It is situated between the sixth and seventh degrees of south latitude, and the 115th and 116th of east longitude.
