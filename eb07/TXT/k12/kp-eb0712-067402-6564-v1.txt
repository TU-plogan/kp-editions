KANANY, a group of small uninhabited islands in the Eastern Seas, lying off the north coast of Mysol, about the 130th degree of east longitude. Good water maybe procured at the south end of the great island. These islands produce a species of nut, full of oil, and about the size of a small almond. The north point of the principal island is fixed in long. 129. 36. E. and lat. 1. 47. S.
