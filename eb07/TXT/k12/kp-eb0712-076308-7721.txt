KOUZNETZK, a small fortified town in Asiatic Russia, and capital of a district of the same name, in the government of Tomsk. It was founded in 1618; and the sable being here of peculiar beauty, the place is a great resort of fur-merchants. Population 500. Long. 87. 30. E. Lat. 53. 20. N.
