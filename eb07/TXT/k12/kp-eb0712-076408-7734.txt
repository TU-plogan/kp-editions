KRESTXY, a city of the province of Novogorod, in Russia, the capital of a circle of the same name, extending over 372 square miles, comprehending one city, 730 villages and hamlets, with 47,387 inhabitants. The city is on the river Khalowa, contains 450 houses, and 1874 inhabitants. Long. 32. 28. E. Lat. 58. 20. N.
