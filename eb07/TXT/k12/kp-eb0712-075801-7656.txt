KOONTASSEE, a small town of Hindustan, in the province of Gujerat and district of Morvec, near the great marshes called the Runn. The country in the neighbourhood is in a deplorable state, and the villages nearly uninhabited, owing to the tumult and confusion which pervade the whole district.
