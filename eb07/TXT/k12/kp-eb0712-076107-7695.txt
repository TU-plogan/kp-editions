KOSZOG, a city of the circle of Eisenburg, in the province of Farther Danube, in the kingdom of Hungary. It is sometimes called Guns, from the river of that name, on which it is built. It has some trade in internal productions, and contains 576 houses, with 5310 inhabitants, mostly of German origin. Long. 16. 27. 58. E. Lat. 47. 22. 54. N.
