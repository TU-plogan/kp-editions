KILLALA, a town of Ireland, in the county of Mayo, situated on a fine bay of the Atlantic, to which it gives name. There are here some small manufactures, but it is chiefly noted as being the place where the French landed in 1798, and of which they kept possession for about a month. It is 192 miles north-west from Dublin. Long. 9. 8. W. Lat. 54. 13. N.
