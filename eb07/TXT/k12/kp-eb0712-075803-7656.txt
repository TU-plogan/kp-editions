KOOTAHE, or Cocoa Island, in the South Pacific Ocean, and separated from Neootabootaboo by a channel only three miles broad. It was discovered by Schouten and Le Maire, on the 10th May 1616. Long. 173. 48. W. Lat. 15. 55. S.
