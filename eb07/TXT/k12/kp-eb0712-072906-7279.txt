KINGSTANLEY, a town in the county of Gloucester, in the hundred of Whitestone, 105 miles from London. It was formerly a borough, and anciently the residence of the Saxon king of Mercia. The inhabitants amounted in 1801 to 1434, in 1811 to 1722, in 1821 to 2269, and in 1831 to 2438.
