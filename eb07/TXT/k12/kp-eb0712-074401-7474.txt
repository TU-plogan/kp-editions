KLOPPENBURG, a circle of the duchy of Oldenburg, in Germany. It extends over 612 square miles, and contains one town and fifteen parishes, with 5023 houses, and 26,964 inhabitants. The chief town, of the same name, is situated on the river Soste, containing 151 houses, and 860 inhabitants; but the whole parish has a population of 4790.
