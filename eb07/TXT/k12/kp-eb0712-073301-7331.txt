KIRILOW, a city of the province of Novogorod, in Russia, the capital of a circle of the same name, which extends over 579 square miles, containing two cities, 1280 villages and hamlets, inhabited by 52,870 persons. The city stands between two fresh-water lakes, and has 620 houses, with 2460 inhabitants. Long. 38. 29. E. Lat. 59. 42. N.
