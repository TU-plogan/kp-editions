KAKREZE, a district of Hindustan, in the province of Gujerat, which consists of eighty-four villages, belonging to several Hindu chiefs. Its principal towns are Therah and Oon, at the latter of which the district commences. Kakreze is fifteen miles to the, north of Rahdunpoor.
