KLIN, a circle in the Russian government of Moscow, which extends over 1347 square miles, comprehending one town and 606 villages, with 8000 houses, and 62,380 inhabitants. The capital, of the same name, is a town situated on the river Sestra, with 1250 inhabitants. Long. 36. 40. E. Lat. 56. 20. N.
