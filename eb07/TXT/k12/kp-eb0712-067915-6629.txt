KARATSCHEW, a city of the province of Orel, in Russia, the capital of a circle of the same name. It stands on the river Sujeshat. It contains twelve churches, three of which are of stone, 1000 houses, and 6230 inhabitants, who make large quantities of twine and ropes. Long. 34. 51. E. Lat. 53. 16. N.
