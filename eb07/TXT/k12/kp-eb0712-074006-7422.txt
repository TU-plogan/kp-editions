Kishenagur, a town and district of Bengal. The town is situated on the south-eastern side of the Jellinghy River. It is the residence of the rajah, and also of the judgecollector of the district of Nuddeah. It is noted for a manufacture of fine cotton cloths. Long. 88. 95. E. Lat. 23. 26. N.
