KREMS, a city of Austria, in the circle of Manharts-berge, and province of the Lou er Ens. It is on the Danube, is well built, and contains a nunnery founded for English females. There are some manufactories of vinegar, of mustard, and of linen goods, and some good wine is made. It contains 424 houses, and 3650 inhabitants. Long. 15. 30. 42. Ä½. Lat. 48. 21. 39. N.
