KUILENBURG, a city of the Netherlands, in the province of Gelders, and circle of Thiel. It stands on the river Leck. It is surrounded with walls, and divided by the river into three parts, which are connected by handsome stone bridges. It is an increasing place, containing, in 1830, 4299 inhabitants, who are occupied in making silk goods, ribbons, and fire-arms.
