KOLIN, a city of the circle of Kaurzim, in the Austrian kingdom of Bohemia. It is on the banks of the Elbe, contains 416 houses, and 4387 inhabitants, who are employed in cotton weaving, and in iron manufactures. Near to it is the field of battle where, in 1757, Daun defeated Frederick of Prussia.
