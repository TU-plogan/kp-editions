KREMSIER, a city of the circle of Prerau, in the Austrian province of Moravia, on the river March. It is the seat of the Archbishop of Olmutz, whose castle is here, with a fine gallery of pictures and a library of 30,000 volumes. It contains, besides the minster, six churches, 420 houses, and 4100 inhabitants, who successfully cultivate vineyards.
