KONIGBERG, a city of the circle of Barsch or Bars, in the Austrian kingdom of Hungary, on the river Gran. It is in a deep valley, surrounded by three mountains. It contains 510 houses, and 3770 inhabitants. There were formerly mines of gold and silver, but these have ceased for several years to be worked. Long. 18. 32. 35. E. Lat. 42. 25. 42. N.
