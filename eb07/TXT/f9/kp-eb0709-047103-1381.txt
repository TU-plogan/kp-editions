FABRIANO, a city of the papal delegation of Macerata, in Italy, on the river Giano. It is the seat of a bishop, has nine monasteries, nine nunneries, and 7224 inhabitants, who are occupied in making paper and preparing parchment. The paper made here is celebrated throughout Italy.
