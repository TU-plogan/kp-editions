FAMILY denotes the persons who live together in one house, under the direction of one head or chief manager. It also signifies the kindred or lineage of a person; and is used by old writers to indicate a hide or portion of land sufficient to maintain one family.

Family, in Natural History, a term used to express any order of animals, or other natural productions, which exhibit certain affinities, or the same characters.

Family Islands, a cluster of small islands near the north-east coast of New Holland, and twelve miles northwest of Cape Sandwich.
