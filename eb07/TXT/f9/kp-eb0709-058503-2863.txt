FINALE, a sea-port in the duchy of Genoa, and kingdom of Sardinia. It is divided into two parts, one called the Hill, the other the Marina. It is strongly fortified, especially against maritime attacks. The inhabitants amount to 3974, who carry on a brisk trade in olives and in oil, which the surrounding district copiously supplies.
