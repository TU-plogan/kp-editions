DIE, an arrondissement in the department of the Drome, in France, 946 square miles in extent. It is divided into nine cantons and 117 communes, and contains 60,518 inhabitants. The capital, a city of the same name, is situated on the right bank of the Drome. It has some silk mills and cloth manufactures, and contains 3421 inhabitants.
