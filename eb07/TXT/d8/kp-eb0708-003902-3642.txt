DIRECT, in Arithmetic, is when the proportion of any terms or quantities is in the natural or direct order in which they stand, being the opposite inverse, which considers the proportion in the inverted order of the terms. So, 3: 4 :: 6: 8 directly; or 3: 4 :: 8: 6 inversely.
