DURADSCH, the ancient Dyrrachium, a sea-port in Greece, in the country of the Arnauts. It is the sec of a Greek archbishop and of a Catholic bishop, is fortified, and contains 5000 inhabitants. There is a good harbour, whence both commerce and piracy have long been carried on. The air is most unwholesome.
