DOABEH Barry is a district of Hindustan, in the [7:8:74]province of Lahore, situated between the Beyah and Ravey rivers, and between the thirtieth and thirty-first degrees of north latitude. Doabeh Jallinder is a large district in the Darne province, between the Sutlege and Beyah rivers, and for the most part between the thirtieth and thirty-first degrees of north latitude.
