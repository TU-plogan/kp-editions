DORY Harbour, a harbour on the northern coast of Papua, At this harbour are neither fowls nor goats; but wild hogs, fish, greens, fruit, &c. Wood is plentiful, and the wild nutmeg grows in the vicinity. Long. 134. 35. E. Lat. 48. S.
