DISAPPOINTMENT, Islands of, a cluster of islands in the Southern Pacific Ocean, so named by Commodore Byron, who discovered them in 1765. The island seen by Lord Byron had a beautiful appearance. The middle of this cluster of islands lies in long. 145.4, W. lat. 14. 5. S.
