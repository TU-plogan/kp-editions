DOBRUSHKA, or Dobruska, a town in the Austrian kingdom of Bohemia, in the circle of Kò’nigingratz, the residence of the princes Colloredo. It has much trade in corn, flax, and yarn, which are largely sold at its weekly market. It contained, in 1819, about 400 houses and 1893 inhabitants.
