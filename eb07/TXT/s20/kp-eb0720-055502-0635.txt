SRI MUTTRA, a considerable town of Hindustan, pro vince of Agra. It is situated on a naked rock of red gra nite, of which material all the houses are constructed. The streets are very narrow, and the working of the red stone into slabs furnishes employment to the greater part of the inhabitants. Long. 77. 20. E. Lat. 26. 41. N.[7:20:556]
