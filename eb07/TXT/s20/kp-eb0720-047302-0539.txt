SOLITARY Island, in the South Pacific ocean, discovered by Mendana in 1595. It is low, and surrounded with rocks. Long. 178. 20. W. Lat. 10. 4. S.

Solitary Islands, a cluster of small islands near the east coast of New Holland. Lat. 30. 8. S.
