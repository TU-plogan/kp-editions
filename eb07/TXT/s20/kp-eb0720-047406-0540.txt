SOLYMANIA, called also Shehr Zaur, a town of Kurdistan, the residence of the Pacha. The town had fallen into decay, but was rebuilt by Solyman the Pacha, who, in 1810, having taken up arms against the Pacha of Bagdad, defeated him and put him to death. Population, 6000; fifty-four miles east of Kerkook.
