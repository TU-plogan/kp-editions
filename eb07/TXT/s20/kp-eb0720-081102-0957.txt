SUPERPARTIENT Proportion, or Ratio, is when the greater term contains the less terra once, and leaves some number greater than 1 remaining: as the ratio of 3 to 5, which is equal to that of 1 to 1 2 / 3 ; of 7 to 10, which is equal to that of 1 to 1 3 / 7 .
