SONDERHAUSEN, the capital of the principality of SchwartzburgSonderhausen, in Germany, stands on the river Wipper, at the junction of the Bebra with that stream. It is well built, and the castle on which the prince resides is situated on a hill overlooking the city; it is a venerable object, with some pleasing promenades near to it. The city contains a gymnasium, two churches, and an orphan house, 460 dwellings, and 3600 inhabitants. Long. 10. 45.
