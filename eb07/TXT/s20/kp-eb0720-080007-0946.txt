SULTANPORE, a town of Hindustan, province of Lahore, belonging to the Sikhs. Long. 74. 45. E. Lat. 31. 18. N. 2. A town in the province of Oude, pleasantly situated on the eastern bank of the Goompty river, seventy-eight miles south east from Lucknow. Long. 82. 3. E. Lat. 26. 18. N. 3. A town in the Afghan territories, province of Mooltan, twenty miles east from the city of Mooltan. Long. 71. 40. E. Lat. 30. 38. N. 4. A town in the Mahratta territories, province of Khandesh, on the north side of the Tuptee river, ninety miles east by north from Surat. Long. 74. 22. E. Lat. 21. 35. N.
