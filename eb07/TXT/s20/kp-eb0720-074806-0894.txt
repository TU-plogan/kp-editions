STRAUBING, a town of the kingdom of Bavaria, in the circle of the Lower Danube, the capital of the bailiwick of the same name. It is finely situated on the bunks of the Danube, over which is a fine bridge. It contains seven churches, four hospitals, a gymnasium and several other schools, 760 houses, and 6800 inhabitants, who trade extensively by the river in corn and live cattle. Long. 11. 29. 28. E. Lat. 48. 52. 39. N.
