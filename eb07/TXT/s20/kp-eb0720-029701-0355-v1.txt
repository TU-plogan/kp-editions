SHIPSTON-on-Stour, a town of the hundred of Oswaldslow, in the county of Worcester, but detached from it, and surrounded by Gloucestershire. It is eighty-three miles from London, lias little trade except what arises from its great sheep-fairs. It has a large market on Friday. The population amounted in 1801 to 1293, in 1811 to 1377, in 1821 to 1562, and in 1831 to 1632.
