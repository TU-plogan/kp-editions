SERRAVALLE, a city of the delegation of Treviso, in the Austrian kingdom of Lombardy. It stands on the river Maschio, between two mountains; is the seat of a bishop, has a cathcdral and several churches and monasteries, and 5630 inhabitants employed in the silk and woollen manufactories, and some trade in corn, wine, and oil.
