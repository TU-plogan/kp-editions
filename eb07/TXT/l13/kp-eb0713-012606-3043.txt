LAUTERBACH, a town of Hesse-Darmstadt, in the canton of Upper Hesse. It is the capital of the bailiwick of its name, is situated on the river Altfell, is walled, and contains 538 houses, with 3050 inhabitants, who are employed in linen and woollen manufactures, and in tanneries.
