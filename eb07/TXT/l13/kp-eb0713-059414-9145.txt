LUND, a city of Sweden, in the province of Malmoe, near the river Hoje-An. It is without walls, and is the seat of a bishop, of a theological seminary, and of a university. There is a respectable cathedral, besides three other churches; and it contains about 400 houses, with 3640 inhabitants.
