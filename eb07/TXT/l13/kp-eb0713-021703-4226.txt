LEIPA, a city of the circle of Leutmcritz, in the Austrian kingdom of Bohemia. It stands on the river Polzen, and has a gymnasium, 688 houses, and 4918 inhabitants. It is a manufacturing place, producing linen and woollen cloths, glass, and earthenware.
