LIGHTER, a large, open, flat-bottomed vessel, generally managed with oars, and employed to carry goods to or from a ship when she is to be laden or delivered. There are also some lighters furnished with a deck throughout their whole length, in order to contain those merchandises which would be damaged by rainy weather. These are usually called close lighters. ∣∙j
