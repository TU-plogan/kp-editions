LETTITSCHEW, a circle of the province Podolia, in Russia. It extends in north latitude from 49. 6. to 49. 37. and in east longitude from 25. 9. to 27. 52. It is well watered by the Bug and the Wotschik, and has many extensive forests, with some fertile soil. The capital, a city of the same name, is 950 miles from Petersburg; it is situated on the Wotschik, and has one Catholic and two Greek churches, 380 houses, and 1860 inhabitants. Long. 27. 39. E. Lat. 40. 22. 30. N.
