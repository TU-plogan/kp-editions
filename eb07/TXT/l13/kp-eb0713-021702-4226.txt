LEINSTER, the eastern province of Ireland, bounded by Ulster on the north; by St George’s, or the Irish Channel, on the east and south; and by the provinces of Connaught and Munster on the west. The capital city of this province and of the kingdom is Dublin. It contains twelve counties, viz. Carlow, Dublin, Kildare, Kilkenny, King’s County, Longford, Louth, Meath, Queen’s County, West Meath, Wexford, and Wicklow.
