RATTOLAW, a seaport of Hindustan, in the province of Gujerat, and gulf of Cambay, is situated on a navigable river. It was ceded to the East India Company by the Guicowar, the Mahratta chief, in 1803. It is forty-four miles south-west from the city of Cambay. Long. 72. 15. E. Lat 22. 3. N.
