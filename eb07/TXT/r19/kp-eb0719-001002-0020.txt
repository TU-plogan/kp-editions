RADE sometimes called Bade by the Forest, a city of the Prussian government of Cleves, in the circle of Len-nep. It is fortified, and having been burned down in 1802, has since been regularly built. It contains 510 houses, with 4950 inhabitants, diligently employed in manufactures of cloths, hosiery, serges, and hardware.
