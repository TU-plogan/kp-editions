REBATE or Rebatement, in Commerce, a term much used at Amsterdam for an abatement in the price of seve-

VOL. XIX.

ral commodities, when the buyer, instead of taking time, advances ready money.

Rebatement, in Heraldry, a diminution or abatement of the bearings in a coat of arms.
