RANDAZZO a city of the district Demone, in the intendency of Messina, in Sicily. It stands on the side of Mount Aetna, looking over a rich and extensive plain, near a curious lake abounding in spunges, the collecting of which forms a part of the employment of the inhabitants. It is 120 miles from Palermo, and moderately healthy. The population amounts to 4700.
