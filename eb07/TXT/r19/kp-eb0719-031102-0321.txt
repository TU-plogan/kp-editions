ROERMOND or Ruremonde, one of the circles into which the province of Limburg is divided. It contains six cantons. The capital is the city of the same name, which stands on the left bank of the Maas, where the river Roer falls into it. It is fortified, but not strongly, and contains 4480 inhabitants, who make woollen cloths and carry on trade by the river. Long. 5. 53.54. E. Lat. 51. 11. 48. N.
