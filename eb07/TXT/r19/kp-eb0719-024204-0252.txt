RIGHT in Geometry, signifies the same with straight; thus, a straight line is called a right one.

Right is a title conferred, 1. together with Reverend, upon all bishops; 2. together with Honourable, upon earls, viscounts, and barons; 3. by courtesy, together with Honourable, upon the sons of dukes, marquises, and the eldest sons of earls; 4. together with Honourable, upon the speaker of the House of Commons, but upon no other commoner excepting those who are members of her majestyâ€™s most honourable privy council, and the three lords mayors of London, York, and Dublin, and the lord provost of Edinburgh, during their office.
