ROCHE-BERNARD a town of France, in the department of Morbihan, and arrondissement of Vannes. It is situated on the river Vilaine, and contains 1380 inhabitants; but within the parish are 6670 persons, who are chiefly employed in making flaxen thread and linen goods.
