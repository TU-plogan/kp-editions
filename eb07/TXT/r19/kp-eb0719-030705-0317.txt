ROCHECHOUART an arrondissement of the department of the Upper Vienne, in France. It extends over 237 square miles, comprehends five cantons, divided into twenty-nine communes, and in 1836 contained 48,818 inhabitants. The capital is the town of the same name, on the banks of the river Grenne. It contains 4123 inhabitants. Long. 0. 45. E. Lat. 45. 43. N. t
