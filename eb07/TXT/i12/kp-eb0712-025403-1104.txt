IMBRO, or Imrus, an island in the Archipelago, to the south-west of the Gulf of Saros, eighty-six square miles in extent. It has 4000 Greek inhabitants, who produce corn, wine, oil, and cotton wool, and occupy one town and several villages. Long. 25. 40. E. Lat. 40. 10. N.
