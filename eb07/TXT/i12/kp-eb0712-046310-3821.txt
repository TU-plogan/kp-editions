ISSURDU, a town of Hindostan, in the province of Asmeer, in the Rajpoot territories. It is surrounded with a wall and ditch, has a citadel in the centre, and is one of the best built towns in the province. Lat. 26° 26' N.; Long. 76° 10' E.
