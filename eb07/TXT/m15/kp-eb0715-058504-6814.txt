MOW is the name of several towns in Hindustan, the principal of which is in the province of Agra, and district ofFurukabad. Long. 79. 18. E. Lat. 27. 34. N. This is also the name of a town in the district of Allahabad, fifty-three miles north-east from Benares; and of another seventy-six miles from Benares.
