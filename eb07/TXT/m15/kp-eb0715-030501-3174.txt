MISTERBIANCO, a town of the island of Sicily, in the kingdom of Naples and the province of Demone, 118 miles from Palermo. It stands upon Mount Aetna, and is the Monasterium Album of Pirro. The situation is healthy, and the inhabitants are 3500. It is surrounded with basaltic rocks and warm baths.
