MUNKACS, a market-town of the circle of Unghvar, in the province of Hither Theiss, in Hungary. It stands on the river Latorcza, and contains a Catholic, a Calvinist, and a Unitarian church, with 730 houses, and 5008 inhabitants, who refine much common salt and saltpetre. Near to it is a celebrated fortress. Long. 22. 16. 58. E. Lat. 48. 25. 48. N.
