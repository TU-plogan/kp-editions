MYO Isle, a small island situated in the Eastern Seas, in the Molucca passage, which separates Celebes and Gilolo. The inhabitants, by whom it was occupied whilst the Portuguese held the Moluccas, were expelled by the Dutch, lest it should become a place for the smuggling of spices. Long. 126. 15. E. Lat. 1. 23.
