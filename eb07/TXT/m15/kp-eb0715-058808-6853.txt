MUCKUNDRA, a village of Hindustan, in the province of Malwah, thirty miles south-south-east from Kotah, being situated in a circular valley at Chundkairy. Fourteen miles from this place is held one of the largest fairs in Hindustan. Long. 76. 12. E. Lat. 24. 48. N.
