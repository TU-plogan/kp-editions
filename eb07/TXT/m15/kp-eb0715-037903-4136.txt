MONADELPHIA (from μονος, alone, and αδsλp∕α, a brotherhood) , a single brotherhood, the name of the sixteenth class in Linnaeus’ sexual system, consisting of plants with hermaphrodite flowers; in which all the stamina are united below into one body or cylinder, through which passes the pistillum. See Botany.
