MORNING, the beginning of the day, or the time of the sun rising. The astronomers reckon morning, mαηe, from the time of midnight to that of mid-day.

Morning Star, the planet Venus when a little to the westward of the sun; that is, when she rises a little before him. In this situation she is called by the Greeks Phosphorus, and by the Latins Lucifer.
