MONA and Monita, that is, The Monkey and his Cub, two islands of the West Indies, in the middle of the great passage between Hispaniola and Porto Rico The Mona Isle measures seven miles one way and two another; and, although once in good cultivation, is now only the solitary range of wild goats.
