MOS, a bailiwick of the province of Aggerhuus, in Norway, containing 19,800 inhabitants. The chief town, of the same name, is situated on the Bay of Christiania, and contains 400 houses, with 1408 inhabitants, who make much bar, rod, and cast iron.
