MINORS, or Friars Minor, an appellation which the Franciscans assume, out of humility, calling themselves fratres minores, or lesser brothers, and sometimes minorités. There, is, also an order of regular minors at Naples, which was established in the year 1588, and confirmed by Pope Sixtùs V.
