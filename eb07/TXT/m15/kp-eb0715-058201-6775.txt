MOULDING, any thing cast in a mould, or which seems to have been so, although in reality it were cut with a chisel or the axe.

Mouldings, in Architecture, projections beyond the naked wall, column, or wainscot, the assemblage of which forms cornices, door-cases, and other decorations of architecture.
