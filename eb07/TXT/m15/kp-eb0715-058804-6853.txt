MUCKEALAH, a town of Hindustan, and province of Lahore, belonging to the Sikhs. The neighbourhood is noted for the abundance of fossil salt. It is eighty-three miles north-west from the city of Lahore. Long. 72. 43. E. Lat. 32. 33. N.
