VIERZON, a city of France, in the department of the Cher, and the arrondissement of Bourges. It stands at the point where the rivers Eure and Eber unite, and contained in 1836, 4706 inhabitants, employed in making hosiery, woollen goods, and leather. Lat. 47. 12. Long. 1. 58. E.
