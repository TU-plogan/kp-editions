JOHANNA, or Anjouan, more properly Hinzouan. See Hinzouan. JOHANN-Georgenstadt, a city of the mining district of the kingdom of Saxony, in the circle of Freyburg, upon the frontiers of Bohemia. It contains 396 houses, and 3000 inhabitants, who are employed in extracting silver, tin, vitriol, sulphur, iron, and manganese, from the surrounding mines.
