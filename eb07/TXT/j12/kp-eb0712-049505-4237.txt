JAHDE, a town of the circle of Neuenburg, in the duchy of Oldenburg, in Germany, on a navigable river of the same name, which empties itself into the sea about two miles below the town. It contains 307 houses, with 2160 inhabitants. Long. 8. 12. 43. E. Lat. 53. 20. 45. N.
