JIGAT Point, a town and promontory'atthe south-west extremity of Gujerat, situated on the Goomty, which is an asylum for pirates, the people of this country being much addicted to piracy, in which they are encouraged by their chiefs. It has a Hindu temple dedicated to Krishna. Long. 69. 7. E. Lat. 22. 12. N.
