The chief place, from which the arrondissement takes its name, is on the right bank of the Lot, which forms almost a circle round it. It contains 1900 houses, and 11,728 inhabitants. There are in it manufactures of paper, cloths, lace, cassimeres, and leather; and some trade in wine, brandy, nut-oil, and other productions, which is increasing. Long. 1. 21. 15. E. Lat. 44. 26. 49.
