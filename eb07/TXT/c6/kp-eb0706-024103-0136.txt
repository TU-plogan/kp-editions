CATECHETIC, or Catechetical, something that relates to oral instruction in the rudiments of Christianity. Catechetic schools were buildings appointed for the office of the catechist, adjoining to the church, and called catechumena ; such was that in which Origen and many other famous men read catechetical lectures at Alexandria.
