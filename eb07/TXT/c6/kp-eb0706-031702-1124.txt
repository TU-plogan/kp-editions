CHANDAHNEE, a small district and town of Hindustan, in the province of Lahore, situated between the 33d and 34th degrees of N. lat. The town is neat and populous, pleasantly situated on the brow of a hill, with a rapid stream running at the foot of it. It is 122 miles north by east from the city of Lahore. Long. 74. 41. E. Lat. 33. 24. N.
