CAPRAIA, a small island of the Sardinian states, lying in the Tuscan Sea, between the north-east point of Corsica and the coast of Piombino. It is about fifteen miles in circumference, and possesses a town of the same name, which has a good harbour. The principal product of the ground is vines. The population amounts to about 2000. Long. 9. 48. 12. E. Lat. 43. 0. 18. N.
