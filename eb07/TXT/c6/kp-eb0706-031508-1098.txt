CHANANAEI, in Ancient Geography, the name of the ancient inhabitants of Canaan in general, descendants of Canaan, but peculiarly appropriated to some one branch; though it is uncertain which it was, or how it happened that they preferred the common gentilitious name to a more appropriate denomination as descendants of one of the sons of Canaan.
