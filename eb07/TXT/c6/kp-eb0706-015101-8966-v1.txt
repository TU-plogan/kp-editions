CAROLINE Islands, or New Philippines, a chain of islands, amounting to thirty, in the Pacific Ocean, which are but little known to Europeans.

Caroline Boohs, the name of four books composed by order of Charlemagne, to refute the second council of Nice. These books are couched in very harsh and severe terms, containing 120 heads of accusation against the council of Nice, and condemning the worship of images.
