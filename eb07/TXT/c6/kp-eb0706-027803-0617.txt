CENEDA, a city of Italy, in the delegation of Treviso, of the Austrian kingdom of Venetian Lombardy. It is situated on the river Maschio, and has, besides the cathedral, several other churches, and 4500 inhabitants, who are employed in woollen and in paper manufactories.
