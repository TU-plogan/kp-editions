CHIPPING-ONGAR, a market-town in the hundred of the same name in Essex, twenty-one miles from London. Here is a castle, built originally by the Romans, upon a mount. The inhabitants amounted in 1801 to 595, in 1811 to 678, and in 1821 to 763. The market is held on Saturday.
