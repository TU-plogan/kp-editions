CHIPPING-SODBURY, a market-town in the hundred of Grumbalƒès Ash, in the county of Gloucester, 113 miles from London, and ten from Bristol. It was formerly a borough, but its charter was revoked at the requisition of the inhabitants. The market is held on Saturday. The population amounted in 1801 to 1090, in 1811 to 1124, and in 1821 to 1168.
