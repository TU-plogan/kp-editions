CICLUT, or Cicluch, a strong frontier town of Dalmatia, situated on the river Narentha, in longitude 18. 22. east, latitude 43. 29. north. It is surrounded with walls built in the ancient manner, and it was taken by the Venetians from the Turks in 1694.
