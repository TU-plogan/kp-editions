CARORA, a city of South America, in the province of Venezuela. It is situated on the river Morera, is well built, and possesses some large and handsome streets. The inhabitants, who amount to about 700, are chiefly engaged in the rearing of productive animals, such as oxen, mules, horses, sheep, and the like. Lat. 9. 50. N.
