CHIAVARI, a city of Italy, in the Sardinian province of Genoa. It is situated in the Bay of Rapallo, where the Sturla empties itself into the sea. It contains 7680 inhabitants, who subsist by the fisheries, and by the cultivation of wine, oil, and silk.
