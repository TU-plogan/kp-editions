CHIUSA, a town of Italy, in the province of Coni, in the kingdom of Sardinia. It stands on the river Pesia, near the ruins of the ancient castle of Mirabella. It contains 6100 inhabitants, who are chiefly employed in the spinning and weaving of silk.
