CESARE, among logicians, one of the modes of the second figure of syllogisms, the minor proposition of which is a universal affirmative, and the other two universal negatives; thus,
Ce
No immoral books ought to be read;
Sa
But every obscene book is immoral;
Re
Therefore no obscene books ought to be read.
