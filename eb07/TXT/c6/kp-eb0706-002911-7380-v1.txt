CALYPSO, in fabulous history, a goddess who was the daughter of Oceanus and Tethys, or, as others say, of [7:6:30] Atlas. She was queen of the island of Ogygia, which from her was called the island of Calypso. According to Homer, Ulysses suffered shipwreck on her coast, and staid with her several years.
