CASTLEDERMOT, or Tristle Dermot, a town of Ireland, in the county of Kildare. It is situated upon the river Lane, on the great southern road from Dublin to Cork. It was anciently a fortified town, and the residence of the kings of Leinster. Here are the remains of an ancient Franciscan abbey, and other antique ruins.
