CATEAU, Le, a city of France, in the department of the North, and arrondissement of Cambray. It stands on the river Selles, and contains 741 houses, with 4133 inhabitants. The finest thread for making lace, and for the finest cambric, is spun here, and occupies a long space of time, as well as the minutest degree of care.
