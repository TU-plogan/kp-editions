CELASTRUS. In Senegal the negroes use the powder of the root of this plant as a specific against gonorrhoeas, which it is said to cure in eight, and sometimes in three days. An infusion of the bark of a species of staff-tree, which grows in the Isle of France, is said to possess the same virtues.
