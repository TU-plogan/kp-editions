ELTMANN, a bailiwick of the circle of the Lower Maine, in the kingdom of Bavaria, extending over forty-four square miles. It contains, besides the capital, thirteen villages and 3039 inhabitants. The capital, of the same name, is situated on the river Zeil, and contains 1010 inhabitants.
