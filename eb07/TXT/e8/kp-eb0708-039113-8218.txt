EAST Island, a small island in the Eastern Seas, near the coast of Palawan. Long. 118. 36. Ä½. Lat. 8. 40. N. Also a small island on the east coast of Borneo. Long. 109. 38. E. Lat. 2. 40. N. Likewise an island in the South Pacific Ocean, on the coast of New Zealand, near Cape East.
