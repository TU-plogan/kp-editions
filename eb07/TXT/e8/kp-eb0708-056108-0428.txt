EILENBURG, a city of the Prussian province of Saxony, in the circle of De)etzsch. It is situated on an island formed by the Mulda, is surrounded with walls, and contains 638 houses, and, with the military, 5100 inhabitants. There are establishments for the printing of calico, and for other manufactures.
