ELBEUF, a city of the department of the Lower Seine, in France, on the left bank of that river, containing 925 houses, and 6550 inhabitants, among whom are many manufacturers of the finest cloth, and of flannels, carpets, and hosiery. Long. 0. 53. E. Lat. 49. 20. N.
