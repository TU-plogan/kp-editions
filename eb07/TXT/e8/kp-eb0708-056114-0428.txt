EISLEBEN, a city of the province of Prussian Saxony, the capital of the circle of Mansfeld. It is divided into the old and the new city, is surrounded with walls, and has several establishments for education, four churches, 957 houses, and 7100 inhabitants. It is celebrated as the birth-place of Martin Luther, the house where he was born being now used as a charity school. It is a place [7:8:562]of considerable trade and manufacturing industry. Long. 11. 26. 5. E. Lat. 51. 53. 5. N.
