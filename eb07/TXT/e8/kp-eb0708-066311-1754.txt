ELENCHUS, in Antiquity, a kind of ear-ring set with large pearls.

Elenchus, in Logic, by the Latins called argumentum and inquisitio, is a vicious or fallacious argument, which deceives under the appearance of a truth, and is the same with what is otherwise called sophism.
