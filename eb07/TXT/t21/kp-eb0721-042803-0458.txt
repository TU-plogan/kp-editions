TURN-AGAIN, a low, flat, and swampy island in Torres Strait, about three miles in length. It is surrounded with reefs, and is overrun with mangroves. Long. 140. 55. E. Lat. 9. 34. S.

Turn-again, Cape, on the east coast of New Zealand, discovered by Captain Cook in 1769. Long. 177. 55. W. Lat. 40. 34. S.
