TCHANG-TCHEOU, a city of China, of the first rank, in Fokien, the most southerly of the province. It iis situated on a river, and carries on a considerable trade. It is 950 miles south of Pekin. Long. 117.34. E. Lat. 24.32. N. This is the name of another city, two leagues in circumference, which is the seat of a very extensive trade, and contains 200,000 inhabitants. It is 525 miles south-south-east of Pekin. Long. 119. 29. E. Lat. 31. 50. N.
