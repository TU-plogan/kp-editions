TCHING-TOU, a city of China, of the first rank, in Sechuen. It was formerly one of the finest cities in the empire, but was almost entirely destroyed in the civil wars in 1646, which preceded the last Tartar invasion. It is 810 miles south-west of Pekin. Long. 103. 44. E. Lat. 30. 40. N.
