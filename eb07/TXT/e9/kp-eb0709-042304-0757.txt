EUTIN, a city in that part of the duchy of Oldenburg which lies on the Baltic Sea, called the duchy of Lubec. It is situated on a beautiful lake, and is surrounded by the Danish province of Holstein. It contains, besides the ducal palace, 260 houses and 2540 inhabitants. Long. 10. 51. E. Lat. 54. 8. N.
