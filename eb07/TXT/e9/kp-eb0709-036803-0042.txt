ESPLANADE, in Fortification, is the sloping of the parapet of the covered-way towards the field, and the same as the glacis of the counterscarp; but it has become in a great measure antiquated in this sense, and is now only applied to the empty space between the glacis of a citadel and the first houses of the town.
