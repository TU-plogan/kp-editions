EPERIES, a city of Hungary, in the circle of the Hither Theiss. It is the seat of the several boards for the government of the circle, and contains 806 houses, and 7426 [7:9:304]inhabitants, who make much linen and woollen goods, and some very excellent wine. Long. 21. 10. 25. E. Lat. 48.
