EYNAPOOR, a town of Hindustan, in the province of Bejapoor, belonging to the Mahrattas, twelve miles southeast from Merritch. It is of considerable size, and contains some Mahommedans, who subsist on grants of charitable lands. Long. 75. 10. E. Lat. 16. 50. N.
