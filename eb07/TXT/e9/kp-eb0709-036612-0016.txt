ESCHWEGE, a city of the province of Lower Hesse, in the principality of Hesse Casel. It is situated on the river Werra, and contains four churches, 782 houses, and 4845 inhabitants, employed in making cloth and tanning leather. It has some navigation on the Werra. In the vicinity a considerable quantity of tobacco is cultivated.
