ASA, king of Judah, succeeded his father Abijam. He j pulled down the altars erected to idols, restored the worship of the true God, and, with the assistance of Benha-dad, king of Syria, took several towns from the king of Israel. He died 917 years before the Christian era, and was succeeded by Jehoshaphat.
