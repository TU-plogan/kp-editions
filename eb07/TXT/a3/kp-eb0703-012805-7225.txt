ANGERS, an arrondissement in the department of the Mayenne and Loire, in France, extending over 436 square miles, or 279,040 acres. It is divided into seven cantons, and those again into 59 communes, containing 92,810 inhabitants. The chief city, of the same name, contains 33,100 inhabitants.
