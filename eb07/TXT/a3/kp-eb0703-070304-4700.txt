ASPER, in Grammar, an accent peculiar to the Greek language, marked thus ('), and importing that the letters over which it is placed ought to be strongly aspirated, or pronounced as if an h were joined with them.

Asper, or Aspre, in Commerce, a Turkish coin, three of which make a medine.
