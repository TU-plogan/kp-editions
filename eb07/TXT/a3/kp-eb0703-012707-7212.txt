ANGELO, Michael. See Buonaroti, Michael Angelo. Angelo, St, a small but strong town of Italy, in the Capitanata. There are several other towns and castles of the same name in Italy, and particularly the castle of St Angelo at Rome. Long. 15. 56. E. Lat. 4L 43. N.
