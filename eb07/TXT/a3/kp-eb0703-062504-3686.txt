AROPH, a contraction of aroma philosophorum, a name given to saffron.

Aroph Paracelsi, a name given to a kind of chemical flowers, probably of the same nature with the Ens Veneris, elegantly prepared by sublimation from equal quantities of lapis haematites and sal ammoniac.
