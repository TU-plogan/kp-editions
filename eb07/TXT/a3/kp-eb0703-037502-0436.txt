ARAUSIO, Civitas Arausiensis or Arausicoum, or Colonia Secundanorum ; so called, because the veterans of the second legion were there settled; now Orange, in the west of Provence, on an arm of the rivulet Egue. An ancient amphitheatre is still to be seen here. Long. 4. 46. E. Lat. 44. 10. N.
