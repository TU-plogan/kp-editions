APINGADAM, a circle in the province of Groningen, in the kingdom of the Netherlands, comprehending four cantons, and containing 40,741 inhabitants. The chief town of the circle bears the same name, is situated on the river Fivel or Damster Diep, and contains 2660 inhabitants.
