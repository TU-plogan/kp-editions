ANTIVARI, or Bar, a strongly fortified town and harbour in the Gulf of Venice, in the Turkish pachalic of Iscander or Scuttari. It is a frontier town, the scat of a Catholic bishop, and contains 3500 inhabitants, who are owners of several vessels, which are hired for the coasting trade of the Adriatic Sea. It is in lat. 42. 25. N. and long. 18. 54. E.
