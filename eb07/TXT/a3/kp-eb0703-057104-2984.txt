ARMAGEDDON, a place spoken of in the Revelation (xvi. 16), which literally signifies the mountain of Maged-don or Megiddo, a city situated in the great plain at the foot of Mount Carmel, where King Josiah received his mortal wound in the battle against Necho, king of Egypt.
