ASOLO, a town of Austrian Italy, in the delegation of Verona, on the river Musone. It is well built and finely situated. It has a cathedral and several other churches, with an ancient castle, and the remains of a Roman aqueduct. The inhabitants are mostly employed in cultivating silk. Long. 12. 1. 5. E. Lat. 45. 47. 37. N.
