ARCHIDAPIFER (from αgχoj, and dapifer, sewer), or chief sewer, is a great officer of the empire. The elector of Bavaria is archidapifcr. The palatine of the Rhine at one time pretended this office was annexed to his palatinate, but he has since desisted.
