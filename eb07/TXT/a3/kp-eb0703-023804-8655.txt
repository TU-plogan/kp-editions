Anstrutner, Wester, a parish and small seaport of Scotland, situated near Laster Anstrutoer, with which, and the towns of Crail, Pittenweem, and Kilrenny, it unites in returning a representative to parliament. The parish includes the Isle of May. There is a weekly packet-boat to Leith; and the steam-boat that plies between Edinburgh and Aberdeen touches at Wester as well as at Easter Anstruther. Population 429. 23 miles north-east of Edinburgh. Long. 2. 44. W. Lat. 56. 12. N.
