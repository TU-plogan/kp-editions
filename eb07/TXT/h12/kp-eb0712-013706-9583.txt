HYPERDULIA, in the Roman Catholic theology, is the worship rendered to the holy virgin. The word is Greek, ŵrsgouÀí/sŕ, composed of wag, above, and 3uλwα, worship, service. The worship offered to saints is called dulia ; and that to the mother of God, hyperdulia, as being superior to the former.
