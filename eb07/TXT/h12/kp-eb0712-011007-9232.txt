HYDROPHYLACIA, a word used by Kircher and some others to express those great reservoirs of water which, as he supposed, were placed in the Alps and other mountains for the supply of the rivers which run through the lower countries. The formation of these reservoirs he considers as one of the great uses of mountains in the economy of the universe.
