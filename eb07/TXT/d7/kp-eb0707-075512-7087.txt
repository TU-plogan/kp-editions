DIAHEXAPLA, or Diahexapte, among farriers, a compound medicine, so called from its containing six ingredients, namely, birthwort and gentian roots, juniperberries, bay-berries, myrrh, and ivory shavings. It is commended for colds, consumptions, pursiness, and many other disorders in horses.
