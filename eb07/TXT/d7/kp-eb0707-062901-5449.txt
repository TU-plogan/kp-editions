DARH, in Logic, one of the modes of syllogism of the first figure, in which the major proposition is an universal affirmative, and the minor and conclusion particular affirmatives: thus,

Every thing that is moved, is moved by another;

Some body is moved;

I Therefore some body is moved by another.
