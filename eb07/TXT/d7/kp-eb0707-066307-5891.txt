DECLA, a town of Hindustan, in the province of Canara, on the coast of Malabar, thirty miles south from Mangalore, near to which are the ruins of an extensive wall or rampart, which divides the province from Malabar. Long. 75. 6. E. Lat. 12. 26. N.
