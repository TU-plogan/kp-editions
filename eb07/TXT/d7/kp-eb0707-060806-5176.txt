DAMGAN, a town of Persia, in the province of Khorassan. It is supposed to have been the ancient Heeatom-pylos, for some time the metropolis of the Parthian empire. It is the chief town of a district of the same name, situated in a spacious plain, famous for a vietory gained by Nadir Shah over the Afghans. Long. 53. 36. E. Lat. 35. 50. N.
