DENDERA, a town of Egypt, on the western side of the Nile. Near it are very magnificent ruins, supposed to be those of an ancient temple of Serapis. It is 48 miles southsouth-east of Girge, and 242 south of Cairo. Long. 31. 40. E. Lat. 26. 10. N.
