DESIDERATUM is used to signify the desirable perfections in any art or science: thus, it is a desideratum with the blacksmjth to render iron fusible by a gentle heat, and yet preserve it hard enough for ordinary uses; with the glassman and looking-glass maker to render glass malleable; and with the clockmaker to bring pendulums to be useful where there are irregular motions.
