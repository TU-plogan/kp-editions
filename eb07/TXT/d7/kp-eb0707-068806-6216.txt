DELMENHORST, one of the circles into which the duchy of Oldenburg, in Germany, is divided. It extends over 380 square miles, and contains 28,460 inhabitants, who subsist by agriculture. The chief town, of the same name, is situated on the river Delme, and contains 242 houses, with 1510 inhabitants.
