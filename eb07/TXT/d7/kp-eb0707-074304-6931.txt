DEUTSCHKRONE, a circle in the Prussian government of Dantzic. It extends over 785 square miles, or 602,400 English acres, comprising five towns and 27,046 inhabitants. The chief place, of the same name, is situated between the two lakes Arnis and Radunsee. It has a Catholic and Lutheran church, with 2015 inhabitants.
