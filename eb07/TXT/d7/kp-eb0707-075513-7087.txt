DIAKOVAR, a market-town of the Austrian Sclavonic province of Siebenbergen. It is the seat of a Catholic bishop and seminary. There is, besides the cathedral, a large Franciscan convent. The population is about 3000. Long. 18. 2. 14. E. Lat. 45. 31. 9. N.
