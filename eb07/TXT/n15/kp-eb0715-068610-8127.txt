NAGY-KAROLI, alarge town of the circle of Szathmar, in the province of Farther Theiss, in Hungary. It is situated on a fruitful spot, and has a splendid palace and gardens belonging to Count Karoli, a Catholic, a Calvinist, and a Unitarian church, a college of philosophy, and a Catholic gymnasium. It contains 943 houses, and 7567 inhabitants, amongst whom are many Jews. Long. 22. 22. 40. E. Lat. 47. 40. 10. N.
