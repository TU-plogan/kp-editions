NAGY-KOBESCH, a city of Hungary, in the district of Pesth. It is composed of 12,000 inhabitants, almost wholly of the original Magyar or Hungarian race, who adhere to the Calvinist church, and are chiefly occupied in agriculture, producing good wine, and rearing large flocks of sheep. Long. 19. 47. E. Lat. 47. 1. N.
