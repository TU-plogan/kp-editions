NARBERTH, a town of South Wales, in the hundred of its own name, in Pembrokeshire, 254 miles from London. It is situated on a hill, contains two townships, and has a market, which is held on Wednesday. The population amounted in 1801 to 1531, in 1811 to 1779, in 1821 to 2395, and in 1831 to 2589.
