SOLICITOR-GENERAL. See Attorney-General. The position of the solicitor-general for Scotland in the main corresponds with that of the English solicitorgeneral. He is next in rank to the lord-advocate. In the United States the office of solicitor-general of the United States was created by Act of Congress in 1870.
