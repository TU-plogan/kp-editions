CURRY, a name applied to a great variety of seasoned dishes. In India the billowing are employed as ingredients in curries :—anise, coriander, cumin, mustard, and poppy seels; allspice, almonds, asafaetida, butter or ghee, cardamoms, chillies, cinnamon, cloves, cocoa-nut and cocoa-nut milk and oil, cream and curds, fenugreek, the tender unripe fruit of Buchanania lancifolia, cheroonjie nuts (the produce of another species, B. latifolia), garlic and onions, [9:6:716] ginger, lime-juice, vinegar, the leaves of Bergera Koenigíi (the curry-leaf tree), mace, mangoes, nutmeg, pepper, saffron, salt, tamarinds, and turmeric.
