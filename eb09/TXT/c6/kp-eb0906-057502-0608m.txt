CREVIER, Jean Baptiste Louis (1693-1765), a French author, was born at Paris, where his father was a printer. He studied under Rollin, and held the professorship of rhetoric in the college of Beauvais for twenty years. He completed Rollin’s Histoire Romaine by the addition of eight volumes; he also published two editions of Livy, with notes; LΗistoire des Empereurs des Romains, jusqu ’ à Constantin, 1749, 12 vols. 12mo; Histoire de V Université de Paris, 7 vols. 12mo; and a Rhétorique Françoise, which enjoyed much popularity.
