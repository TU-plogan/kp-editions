CALITRI, a town of Italy, in the province of Principato Ulteriore and district of Sant’ A,ngelo de’ Lombardi, about 40 miles S.E. of Benevento. It is situated on an eminence near the River Ofanto, and is supposed to occupy the site of an ancient town called Aletrium. Its principal buildings are the parish church and a Benedictine convent. Population, 6629
