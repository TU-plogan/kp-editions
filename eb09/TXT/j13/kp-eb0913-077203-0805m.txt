JUMET, or Jumetz, a town and commune of Belgium, in the arrondissement of Charleroi and the province of Hainault, is situated about 4 miles north-east of Charleroi. Its manufactures include glass-bottles, knives, nails, and hats; and there are extensive coal-mines in the neighbourhood. The population of the commune in 1876 was s 20,102.
