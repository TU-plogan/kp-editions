MUNCIE, a city of the United States, county seat of Delaware county, Indiana, 54 miles north-east of Indianapolis. It is a flourishing pleasant-looking place of 5219 inhabitants (census 1880), with city-hall, court-house, public library, and considerable industrial activity.
