ARAHAL, a town in Spain, 7 miles from Seville. Situated on the level summit of a hill, it enjoys a beautiful prospect. In the wars against the Moors, it assisted the mother-city of Moron, and received its civic independence in 1555 by charter from Charles V. Its church is of Gothic architecture. The country around produces wheat, barley, beans, pease, tares, and olives. Population about 9287. .
