ANTONINUS LIBERALIS, a Greek grammarian, who probably lived about 147 a.d. His work, μeτaμopφωσeωv συναγωγή, consists of forty-one tales of mythical metamorphoses, and is chiefly valuable for the study of Greek mythology. One of the best editions is that of Koch, Leipsic, 1832, 8vo.
