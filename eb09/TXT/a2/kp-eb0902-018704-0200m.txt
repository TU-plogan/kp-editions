APOLLONIUS of Tralles and his brother Tauriscus were the sculptors of the famous Farnese Bull, a group representing Zethus and Amphion tying the revengeful Dirce to the tail of a wild bull. This work is now at Naples. There were several other sculptors named Apollonius.
