REFORMED CHURCHES the designation of those Protestant bodies who adopted the tenets of Zwingli (and later of Calvin), as distinguished from those of the Lutheran or Evangelical divines. Compare Presbyterianism, Re formation, and Germany, vol. x. p. 469.
