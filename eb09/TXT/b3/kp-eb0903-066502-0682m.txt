BIDA, an inland town of Africa, situated in about N. lat. 9° 5' and E. long. 6° 5', sixteen miles N. of the Biver Niger or Quorra, and lying N.N.W. of the town of Egga. Bida, which was visited by Dr Baikie in 1862, is a large town, the capital of the kingdom of Nupe.
