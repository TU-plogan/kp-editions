BRUTON, a market-town of England, in the county of Somerset, pleasantly situated in the valley of the Brue, about 10 miles S.S.W of Frome by rail. It has a townhall, a hexagonal market-cross, a grammar school, and some manufactures of hair-cloth, woollens, and hosiery. Population of hundred in 1871, 3701.
