BRA, a town of Italy in the province of Cuneo and district of Alba, on the River Stura, 25 miles N.E. of Cuneo. It has three parish churches, a gymnasium, a hospital, manufactures of silk and linen goods, and a considerable trade in corn, cattle, wine, and silk. Population, 12,946.
