BUJALANCE, a town of Spain, in the province of Cordova, and about 25 miles E. of that city. It contains a Moorish castle flanked with towers, two hospitals, a foundling asylum, and a seminary for the education of girls. Leather and woollen cloth are manufactured. Population 8946.
