FÜNFHAUS, Fünfhäusel, formerly Hangendenlissen, a flourishing and populous suburb to tho south-west of Vienna, forming, along with Sechshaus proper and Rudolfsheim, the commissariat of Sechshaus, aud trending in a westerly direction towards Schönbrunn. The principal manufactures are silk, satin, velvet, and cotton fabrics. Hangendenlissen was a village belonging to the Barnabito college of St Michael at Vienna. The population, which in 1869 numbered 27,065, had increased in 1872 to 36,388.
