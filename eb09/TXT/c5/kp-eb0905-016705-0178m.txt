CARVIN, a town of France, in the department of Pas-de-Calais, 14½ miles E.S.E. of Béthune. It is a flourishing centre of industry, and carries on a large manufacture of beetroot sugar, alcohol, and starch. Population of the town in 1872, 5780, and of the commune, 7024.
