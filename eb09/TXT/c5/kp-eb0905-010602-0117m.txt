CARINOLA, a town of Italy, in the province of Terra di Lavoro, a district of Gaeta, in 41° 11' 16' ’ N. lac. and 13° 58' 32* E. long. It was formerly the see of a bishop, and is believed to occupy the site of the Lombard city of Foro Claudio, which was founded in 1058. Its principal buildings are the cathedral, the convents, and the seminary. Population, 7640,
